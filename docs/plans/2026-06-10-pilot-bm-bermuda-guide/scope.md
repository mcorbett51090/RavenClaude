# FORGE G0 — Scope: Pilot.BM (high-end Bermuda guide)

**Owner:** Matt. **Repo:** `Pilot.BM` (new, separate from RavenClaude). **Date:** 2026-06-10.

## Scoped intent (one paragraph)

Build **Pilot.BM**, a high-end, design-forward Bermuda guide serving **three audiences** — **Visitors**, **People moving to Bermuda (Movers)**, and **Residents** — each with tailored, curated editorial content, launching **all three audiences at once**. Monetize via a **blended model recommended from realistic-traffic economics** (not assumed) so the site can **pay for its own running + content costs**.

## Owner's locked answers (G0)
- **Revenue:** blend — the plan models 2–3 streams against real Bermuda traffic and recommends the mix.
- **Launch scope:** all 3 audiences at launch.
- **Content:** curated editorial we write (high-end differentiator; budget the maintenance burden).

## Success signal
A live, on-brand 3-audience site with curated launch content + at least one working revenue stream and a **credible, evidenced path to covering its own hosting + content costs** (the research says this is *marginal* and depends on owner-written content + directory sales — the plan must be honest about that).

## Out of scope (initial)
- Our own booking engine (use affiliates/links). Native mobile app. Non-Bermuda destinations. Heavy user accounts beyond what monetization needs at MVP. Paid concierge *fulfillment* service (the premium tier is an upside lever, not the base case).

## Owner constraints baked in
- **Legal:** Movers content must never be legal/immigration/tax advice — disclaimers + "verify at gov.bm" on every regulated page.
- **Economics:** the plan must NOT assume "small ads pay for it." Directory listings + owner-written content is the evidenced path; ads/affiliates are supplementary.
