## Format rules (don't change them)

- **≤ 1 page.** If it's longer, it's not a status report — it's a project summary. Trim.
- **Same seven elements, same order, every week.** Consistency is what makes status reports trusted.
- **Newer reports above older reports** in `status-history.md`. Don't overwrite — append.
- **Audience tailoring lives in tone, not structure.** A 🟢/🟡/🔴 rating means the same thing whoever is reading. The *language* shifts (more formal for client, more direct internally) but the seven sections don't move.
