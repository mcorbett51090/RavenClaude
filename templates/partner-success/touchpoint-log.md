## Convention

- One section per touchpoint, newest at top.
- **30-day silence rule:** if the most recent entry is >30 days old on an active partner, the agent will surface it for action. Silent partners drift quietly.
- Brief entries — 4 lines is typical. If a touchpoint requires more space, it probably belongs in the QBR or as a success-plan update.
- Cross-reference QBR action items: when an action item from a QBR completes, log the closing touchpoint here with `(closes Q2-A1)` or similar.
