## Convention

- One QBR per partner per quarter. Lives at `docs/partner-success/<partner-slug>/qbrs/<YYYY-Qn>.md`.
- **Prep starts 1 week ahead, minimum.** Last-minute QBRs are theater.
- Same six sections, every quarter, same order. Consistency is what makes a QBR a real review and not just a status update.
- After the meeting: convert the action items into touchpoint-log entries with their owners and due dates.
