**Target completion:** YYYY-MM-DD *(default: 90 days)*

---

## Week 1 — Welcome & alignment

- [ ] Welcome email sent (with intro to PSM team and what to expect)
- [ ] Kickoff call scheduled within first 5 business days
- [ ] Partner profile drafted (`profile.md`)
- [ ] Their goals captured — what does success look like *in their words?*
- [ ] **Compliance checkpoint:** state DPA / FERPA acknowledgment confirmed
- [ ] Internal team introduced (CSM, Sales, Support, Solutions Engineer if applicable)

## Weeks 2–3 — Demo & setup *(the high-touch sweet spot)*

- [ ] **Live demo session** with partner stakeholders — full feature walkthrough
- [ ] **Rostering walkthrough** — discuss their SIS, agree on standard (OneRoster / Clever / ClassLink / manual), schedule first sync
- [ ] **District IT alignment meeting** — get IT/security buy-in early; surface firewall, SSO, and data-flow concerns
- [ ] **First translation test** — pick 1–2 languages they care about; validate quality together
- [ ] **Admin configuration session** — set up roles, permissions, branding *with* them, not *for* them
- [ ] **First-content launch** — push first parent communication or first-week schedule together

## Weeks 4–6 — Validation & reinforcement

- [ ] Integration validated end-to-end (rostering syncing cleanly, no errors)
- [ ] Power-users identified and given direct PSM contact info
- [ ] **Common-issues runbook shared** — proactive, not reactive — the *"things people usually trip on"* list
- [ ] First success-metric checkpoint (early indicator of adoption)
- [ ] Health scorecard initialized

## Weeks 8–12 — Habit formation

- [ ] First success plan drafted *with* partner (`success-plan.md`)
- [ ] Cadence agreed: monthly check-ins, quarterly QBRs
- [ ] First QBR scheduled
- [ ] **Onboarding retro:** what worked, what was painful, what to fix for the next partner
- [ ] **Lessons captured** — anything non-obvious gets logged to `lessons-learned.md`

## Sign-off

- [ ] Partner confirms onboarding is complete
- [ ] Internal team confirms partner is in steady-state operating mode
- [ ] Health scorecard at 🟢 Green for two consecutive months before declaring "onboarded"

---

## Convention

- **High-touch by design** — the support-ticket reduction comes from upfront time investment.
- Lives at `docs/partner-success/<partner-slug>/onboarding.md`.
- Checkboxes are the artifact — when they're all checked, the partner moves into steady-state operations.
- The onboarding retro at the end feeds the lessons-learned file. Each partner's onboarding teaches something for the next.
