# Skill: Read outcomes segmented

A school average can rise while a subgroup falls (§3 #6).

## Step 1 — Pull the outcome metric
Proficiency and growth for the period.

## Step 2 — Disaggregate
By grade, subgroup, and program — surface a falling group (§3 #6).

## Step 3 — Link to attendance
The chronic-absenteeism signal behind the achievement (§3 #2).

## Step 4 — Target the intervention
To the largest gap; route special-ed to counsel (§2).

## Output
A segmented outcome read naming the falling subgroup and the attendance link.
