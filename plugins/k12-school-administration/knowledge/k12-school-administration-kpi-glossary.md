# K-12 School Administration KPI Glossary

> The team's canonical metric definitions. Every metric carries a **definition**, a **window**, and a **baseline** before it ships (CLAUDE.md §3 #1). Benchmark ranges are `[unverified — training knowledge]` unless a dated source is attached — confirm against a current source before using in a deliverable (§3 #8).

## Enrollment & attendance

| Metric | Definition | Window | Note |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Enrollment** | Students registered | Census-day + rolling | The funded base; manage the pipeline (§3 #1). |
| **ADA rate** | Average daily attendance ÷ enrolled | Period | Funding + outcome lever (§3 #2). |
| **Chronic absenteeism** | Students at/over the absence threshold ÷ enrolled | Rolling | Early-warning signal; flag early (§3 #5). |
| **Retention / re-enrollment** | Students retained year-to-year | Annual | Mid-year attrition erodes the funded base (§3 #1). |

## Staffing & budget

| Metric | Definition | Window | Note |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Student:teacher ratio** | Enrollment ÷ teaching FTE | Point-in-time | Must fit the budget envelope (§3 #3). |
| **Per-pupil funding** | Funding ÷ enrollment | Year | The primary resource lever (§3 #4). |
| **Salary cost** | Teaching FTE × avg loaded cost | Year | Largest controllable line (§3 #3). |
| **Teacher retention** | Teachers retained year-to-year | Annual | A budget + outcome lever (§3 #7). |

## Outcomes

| Metric | Definition | Window | Note |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Proficiency** | Students at/above standard | Period, by subgroup | Read segmented, not averaged (§3 #6). |
| **Growth** | Year-over-year achievement change | Annual, by subgroup | An average can hide a falling group (§3 #6). |
| **Attendance-achievement link** | Chronic absence vs proficiency | Rolling | Attendance predicts achievement (§3 #2). |

## The rule

A metric without a **window** and a **baseline** is not a finding — it's a number (§3 #1). A benchmark without a **source URL + retrieval date** is `[unverified — training knowledge]` (§3 #8).
