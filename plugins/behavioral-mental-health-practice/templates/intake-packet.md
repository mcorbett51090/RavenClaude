# Intake Packet Template

> **Usage:** Copy this template into the consumer project and customize for the practice. This is an
> **operational framework** — every section should be reviewed by the practice's compliance officer
> or legal counsel before patient use. Clinical content is the responsibility of the licensed clinician.
> HIPAA and 42 CFR Part 2 requirements are US-specific; confirm current regulatory status at use.

> *Note for practice:* If the practice offers substance-use disorder (SUD) services under a federally
> assisted program, a separate **42 CFR Part 2 Notice** must be provided in addition to the HIPAA
> notice. Part 2-protected records require specific written consent for disclosure; HIPAA's TPO
> carve-out does NOT apply. See Section 6 and `skills/behavioral-billing-and-authorization/SKILL.md`.
