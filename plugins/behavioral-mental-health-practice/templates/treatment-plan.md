## Treatment Plan Audit Checklist (for compliance review)

- [ ] Presenting problem stated in functional terms
- [ ] Functional impairment documented in at least one domain
- [ ] Diagnosis with ICD-10 code present
- [ ] Medical necessity statement complete and explicit
- [ ] At least one MBC instrument with initial score documented
- [ ] At least two SMART goals with measurable targets and target dates
- [ ] Interventions listed and linked to goals
- [ ] Frequency and estimated duration documented
- [ ] Clinician signature and date present
- [ ] Plan updated within the last 90 days (or authorization cycle)
