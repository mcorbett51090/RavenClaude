# Skill: Audit attribution data

Duplicate leads and broken UTMs corrupt every number downstream (§3 #7).

## Step 1 — Check dedup
Duplicate-lead rate; above a few percent it corrupts funnel and CAC math (§3 #7).

## Step 2 — Check UTM coverage
Untagged or inconsistently-tagged touches that break attribution (§3 #2).

## Step 3 — Find orphaned touches
Touch records with no contact/opp linkage.

## Step 4 — Gate analysis
Don't trust conversion or ROI reads until hygiene clears (§3 #7).

## Output
A data-integrity report gating downstream analysis. Traverse Tree 3 in the decision-trees file.
