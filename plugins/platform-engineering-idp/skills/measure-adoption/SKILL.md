# Skill: Measure adoption

A feature nobody adopts is negative value (§3 #7).

## Step 1 — Define on-path
What counts as a team being on the golden path — concrete, not aspirational.

## Step 2 — Compute the ratio
Teams on path ÷ total via `platform_engineering_idp_calc.py adoption` (§3 #7).

## Step 3 — Name the gap
The un-adopted teams are the backlog; segment them by why.

## Step 4 — Tie to friction
Route each gap cluster to a golden-path friction or a missing capability (§3 #2).

## Output
An adoption ratio with the gap named and the un-adopted teams segmented.
