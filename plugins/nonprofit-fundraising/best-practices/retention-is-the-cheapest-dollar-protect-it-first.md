# Retention is the cheapest dollar — protect it first

**Status:** Absolute rule. **Constitution:** §3, §4.

## Use when
Any nonprofit fundraising deliverable where this question is in play.

## The rule
It costs ~$0.20 to retain a donor vs ~$1.50 to acquire one; overall donor retention sits ~43–45% and repeat donors generate ~61.6% of all dollars. A leaky bucket isn't fixed by pouring in faster.

## Why
This is house opinion §3 #1. Getting it wrong is the most common way a nonprofit fundraising analysis misleads the operator who acts on it.

## How to apply
- Apply it before reaching for a method.
- Make the relevant number carry a definition, window, and baseline (§3 #1).
- Cite the source + date for any external figure, or mark it `[unverified — training knowledge]`.
