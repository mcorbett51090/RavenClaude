# Skill: Segment the donor base

Treating a $25 first-timer like a $25k loyal donor wastes both.

## Step 1 — Score recency, frequency, value
Build RFM-style segments (§3 #3).

## Step 2 — Tier the base
Group into acquisition, retention, and major-gift tiers.

## Step 3 — Assign the strategy
Match each tier to a channel and cultivation intensity.

## Step 4 — Direct the hours
Concentrate staff time on the highest-value movable segments.

## Output
An RFM segmentation, donor tiers, a per-tier strategy, and an hours allocation.
