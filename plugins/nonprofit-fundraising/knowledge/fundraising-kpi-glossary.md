# Fundraising KPI glossary

The metrics a development office is judged on — formulas, the misreads, and where a benchmark needs a date.

## Donor metrics

Donor retention (overall ~43–45%), new vs repeat retention, donor count, average gift, RFM segments. Retention is the cheapest dollar (§3 #1).

## Efficiency metrics

Cost-to-raise-a-dollar by channel, return on fundraising investment, gift-processing cost (§3 #4).

## Pipeline metrics

Grant win rate, major-gift pipeline value, moves-management velocity, restricted/unrestricted mix (§3 #5, #6).
