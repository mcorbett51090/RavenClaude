# Fundraising benchmarks (2025–2026)

Dated benchmarks for a development read. Donor counts have fallen since 2021 — every figure carries a source and a retrieval date.

## Retention

Overall donor retention edged from ~43.1% to ~43.3% by Q4 2025; the average hovers ~45% (top performers 60%+). First-time retention ~20–30% vs repeat ~60–70%; repeat donors give ~3x more and generate ~61.6% of all dollars (§3 #1).

## Cost to raise a dollar

~$0.20 to retain a donor vs ~$1.50 to acquire one — over 7x more to find a new donor than to keep one (§3 #4).

## Donor counts

Donor counts fell an estimated ~3.6% in 2025, extending a decline since 2021; the smallest gifts ($1–$100), ~57% of donors, dropped ~11.1% YoY (§3 #8).
