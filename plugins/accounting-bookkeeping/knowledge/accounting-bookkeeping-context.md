## Where defensible benchmarks come from

DSO/DPO, cash-conversion-cycle, and days-to-close benchmarks are **industry-, size-, and date-specific** and move year to year. Practitioners cite industry working-capital surveys and close-cycle studies, but the most defensible source is the client's own trailing ledger history. **Name the source and date, or mark the figure `[unverified — training knowledge]` (§3 #8).**

## Operating rhythm

- **Close** monthly on a cadence with a days-to-close target and critical-path checklist (§3 #1).
- **Reconciliation** every period before any statement ships (§3 #2).
- **Working-capital review** monthly: DSO/DPO/DIO and the cash cycle (§3 #3 #4).
- **Controls + COA review** quarterly: SoD, approval thresholds, COA hygiene (§3 #5 #7).
