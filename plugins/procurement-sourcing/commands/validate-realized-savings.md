## Steps (traverse top-to-bottom; do not skip)
1. Set the baseline — A baseline finance recognizes — prior price/volume (§3 #3).
2. Measure realized — Actual spend vs baseline at the new rate.
3. Locate leakage — Maverick spend, volume miss, price creep, or scope change.
4. Report to the P&L — Reconcile realized savings to a finance-validated number.
