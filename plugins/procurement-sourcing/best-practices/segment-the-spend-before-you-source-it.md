# Segment the spend before you source it

**Status:** Absolute rule. **Constitution:** §3, §4.

## Use when
Any procurement & sourcing deliverable where this question is in play.

## The rule
Not every category deserves the same play; the Kraljic matrix (supply risk × spend) tells you whether to leverage, partner, secure, or simplify. Running an auction on a strategic single-source category destroys value.

## Why
This is house opinion §3 #1. Getting it wrong is the most common way a procurement & sourcing analysis misleads the operator who acts on it.

## How to apply
- Apply it before reaching for a method.
- Make the relevant number carry a definition, window, and baseline (§3 #1).
- Cite the source + date for any external figure, or mark it `[unverified — training knowledge]`.
