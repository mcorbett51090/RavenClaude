# Supplier risk is a portfolio, not a checkbox

**Status:** Pattern. **Constitution:** §3, §4.

## Use when
Any procurement & sourcing deliverable where this question is in play.

## The rule
Financial, operational, geographic, and concentration risk across the supply base is a managed portfolio; a single-source critical part with no mitigation is an unpriced liability.

## Why
This is house opinion §3 #4. Getting it wrong is the most common way a procurement & sourcing analysis misleads the operator who acts on it.

## How to apply
- Apply it before reaching for a method.
- Make the relevant number carry a definition, window, and baseline (§3 #1).
- Cite the source + date for any external figure, or mark it `[unverified — training knowledge]`.
