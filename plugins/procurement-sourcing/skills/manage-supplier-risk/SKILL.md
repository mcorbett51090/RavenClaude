# Skill: Manage supplier risk as a portfolio

A single-source critical item with no mitigation is an unpriced liability.

## Step 1 — Score supplier risk
Financial, operational, geographic risk by supplier (§3 #4).

## Step 2 — Map concentration
Spend and criticality concentration across the base.

## Step 3 — Find the single-source exposure
Critical items with no qualified alternative.

## Step 4 — Design mitigation
Dual-source, buffer stock, or qualification, sized to exposure.

## Output
A supplier-risk portfolio, the concentration map, the single-source exposure, and a mitigation plan.
