# Skill: Source on total cost of ownership

A price savings that raises TCO is a loss in disguise.

## Step 1 — Build the TCO model
Unit price plus freight, quality, switching, inventory, lifecycle (§3 #2).

## Step 2 — Compare bids on TCO
Rank suppliers on total cost, not headline price.

## Step 3 — Stress the assumptions
Test the non-price drivers that swing TCO.

## Step 4 — Recommend on total cost
Choose the lowest defensible TCO, not lowest price.

## Output
A TCO model, a TCO-ranked bid comparison, and a total-cost recommendation.
