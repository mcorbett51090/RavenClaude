# Skill: Build the spend cube

You can't source what you can't see.

## Step 1 — Aggregate the spend
Pull and unify spend across systems and units (§3 #5).

## Step 2 — Classify it
Map spend to a clean category taxonomy and normalize suppliers.

## Step 3 — Surface tail spend
Isolate the fragmented tail where savings hide.

## Step 4 — Prioritize
Rank categories by addressable savings.

## Output
A classified spend cube, surfaced tail spend, and a savings-prioritized category list.
