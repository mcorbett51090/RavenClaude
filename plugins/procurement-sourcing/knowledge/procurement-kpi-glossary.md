# Procurement KPI glossary

The metrics a procurement function is judged on — formulas, the misreads, and where a benchmark needs a date.

## Savings metrics

Negotiated vs realized savings, cost avoidance, savings against a finance baseline, spend under management. Only realized savings counts (§3 #3).

## Sourcing metrics

Total cost of ownership, should-cost variance, spend-cube coverage, tail-spend share, category Kraljic placement (§3 #1, #2, #5).

## Risk & supplier metrics

Supplier risk score, supply-base concentration, single-source exposure, on-time/quality performance (§3 #4).
