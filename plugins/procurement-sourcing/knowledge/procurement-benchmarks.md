# Procurement methods & benchmarks (2025)

Reference frameworks plus dated CPO benchmarks for a sourcing engagement. Method references are timeless; market figures carry a source + date.

## Frameworks

The Kraljic matrix (supply risk × spend) for category segmentation; TCO and should-cost for sourcing leverage; the spend cube for visibility (§3 #1, #2, #5). These are method references, not market figures.

## Savings & spend-under-management (Deloitte 2025 CPO)

Modern teams target ~3–7% annual hard savings, and highly strategic teams manage 80%+ of total spend; ~79% of CPOs name procurement savings their top priority. Realized savings must reconcile to a finance-recognized baseline — negotiated savings is a forecast, not a result (§3 #3).

## Tail/maverick spend

An estimated ~20–30% of spend falls into unmanaged tail spend, the most common leakage source; policy-guided buying steers purchases on-contract to cut maverick spend (§3 #5). Spend visibility precedes strategy — you can't source what you can't see.

## Market figures

Commodity indices, market rates, and supplier financials are time-sensitive — every such figure in a deliverable carries a source + date or is marked `[unverified — training knowledge]` (§3 #8).
