# Procurement methods & benchmarks

Reference frameworks for a sourcing engagement. Market indices move constantly — date every external figure or mark it `[unverified — training knowledge]`.

## Frameworks

The Kraljic matrix (supply risk × spend) for category segmentation; TCO and should-cost for sourcing leverage; the spend cube for visibility (§3 #1, #2, #5). These are method references, not market figures.

## Savings discipline

Realized savings must reconcile to a finance-recognized baseline; negotiated savings is a forecast, not a result (§3 #3). Maverick spend and tail spend are the most common leakage sources.
