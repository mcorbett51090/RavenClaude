# Risk drives controls, not the reverse

Start from a risk register — assets, threats, likelihood × impact — then select controls that treat the top risks. A control catalogue assembled by working down a framework checklist, with no risk behind each control, produces cost without benefit and misses the exposures the checklist didn't anticipate. Conversely, a top risk with no control is the org's real liability. Score the register, choose a treatment per risk (mitigate, accept, transfer, avoid), trace each control back to the risk it treats, and track residual risk.
