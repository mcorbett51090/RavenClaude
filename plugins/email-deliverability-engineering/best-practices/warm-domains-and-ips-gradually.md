# Warm new domains and IPs gradually, engaged-recipients-first

**Rule.** New sending domains/IPs have no reputation. Ramp volume gradually and
send to your most-engaged recipients first. There is no shortcut.

**Why.** Full volume on day one looks like a spam attack; engagement is what
builds reputation, so warm against the recipients most likely to open.

**Smell.** A brand-new IP blasting full production volume on day one.
