# Cite the standard, or mark the claim unverified

**Rule.** Every record/header recommendation names its RFC. Every
mailbox-provider threshold (complaint rate, volume cutoff, unsubscribe window)
carries a source + date, or is marked `[unverified — training knowledge]` and
verified before it enters a customer-facing deliverable.

**Why.** RFC mechanics are durable; provider policy is volatile. Conflating the
two ships confidently-wrong platform facts — the failure mode the marketplace
accuracy discipline exists to prevent.

**Smell.** "Gmail requires X%" stated with no date and no source.

**Cite:** AGENTS.md accuracy discipline; plugin §4.9.
