# Site activation is the schedule's long pole

**Status:** Pattern. **Constitution:** §3, §4.

## Use when
Any clinical trials deliverable where this question is in play.

## The rule
Site selection, contracting, and activation gate the timeline; ~80% of trials are delayed at least a month, and delay can cost ~$0.6M–$8M per day in lost opportunity.

## Why
This is house opinion §3 #4. Getting it wrong is the most common way a clinical trials analysis misleads the operator who acts on it.

## How to apply
- Apply it before reaching for a method.
- Make the relevant number carry a definition, window, and baseline (§3 #1).
- Cite the source + date for any external figure, or mark it `[unverified — training knowledge]`.
