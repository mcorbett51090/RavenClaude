# Clinical trial benchmarks (2025–2026)

Dated cost and timeline benchmarks. Figures vary widely by phase and therapeutic area — every figure carries a source and a retrieval date.

## Cost

Trials run ~$4M (Phase I) to $100M+ (Phase III); per-patient cost ~$113,000–$136,000. Recruitment/retention is ~20–30% of budget; the split is roughly sites ~30%, recruitment ~20%, data management ~15%, regulatory ~10%, other ~25% (§3 #2, #6).

## Recruitment & retention

~$6,533 to recruit a patient; ~$19,533 to replace one lost to non-compliance. Average dropout ~30%; over two-thirds of sites miss enrollment and up to 50% enroll one or no patients (§3 #1, #3).

## Delay

~80% of trials are delayed at least a month; delay can cost ~$0.6M (potentially up to ~$8M) per day in lost opportunity (§3 #4).
