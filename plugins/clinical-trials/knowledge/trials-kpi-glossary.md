# Clinical trial KPI glossary

The operational metrics a trial is judged on — formulas, the misreads, and where a benchmark needs a date.

## Enrollment metrics

Screening rate, screen-fail rate, enrollment rate vs plan, the recruitment funnel, sites enrolling zero/one. Enrollment is a rate, not a count (§3 #5).

## Retention & cost metrics

Dropout rate (~30% average), per-patient cost (~$113k–$136k), recruitment cost (~$6,533) and replacement (~$19,533) (§3 #2, #3).

## Schedule metrics

Site-activation cycle time, first-patient-in, last-patient-in, and submission-readiness (§3 #4, #7).
