# Skill: Plan the recruitment funnel

Recruitment is a costed pipeline (~$6,533/patient).

## Step 1 — Define the funnel
Screened → screen-failed → enrolled → retained, with conversion at each stage (§3 #5).

## Step 2 — Cost each stage
Cost per referral, screen, and enrollment against the ~20–30% budget share (§3 #2).

## Step 3 — Size the channels
Plan referral/outreach channels to hit the enrollment target.

## Step 4 — Build in retention
Plan retention to avoid ~$19,533 replacements (§3 #3).

## Output
A costed recruitment funnel, channel sizing, and a retention plan.
