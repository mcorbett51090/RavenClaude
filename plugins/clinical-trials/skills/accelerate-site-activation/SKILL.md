# Skill: Accelerate site activation

Activation is the schedule's long pole; delay can cost $0.6M–$8M/day.

## Step 1 — Map the activation steps
Selection, contracting, regulatory/IRB, and start-up (§3 #4).

## Step 2 — Find the bottleneck
Locate the step delaying first-patient-in.

## Step 3 — Parallelize where possible
Overlap contracting and start-up tasks.

## Step 4 — Recover the schedule
Build the activation-recovery plan against the timeline.

## Output
An activation-step map, the bottleneck, a parallelization plan, and a schedule recovery.
