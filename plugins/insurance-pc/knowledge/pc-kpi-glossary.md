# P&C insurance KPI glossary

The metrics a P&C book is judged on — formulas, the misreads, and where a benchmark needs a date.

## Result metrics

Combined ratio (loss + expense), loss ratio, expense ratio, NCR by line. Under 100 is underwriting profit; read loss and expense separately (§3 #1).

## Loss metrics

Frequency, severity, attritional vs catastrophe loss ratio, loss development, reserve adequacy (§3 #3, #4, #5).

## Pricing & claims metrics

Rate adequacy / indicated rate, indemnity leakage, LAE, claims cycle time (§3 #2, #7).
