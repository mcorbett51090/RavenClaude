# P&C underwriting economics

How a P&C book makes money — the combined ratio, rate adequacy, frequency/severity, cat, and reserves.

## The combined ratio

Loss + expense; strip cat to judge the attritional book (§3 #1, #4).

## Rate adequacy

Price to expected loss + expense + profit, not the competitor (§3 #2).

## Claims & reserves

Leakage and cycle time are managed; reserves are the truth-teller (§3 #5, #7).
