# P&C industry results (2025–2026)

Dated underwriting-result context. Results swung to a decade best in 2025 — every figure carries a source and a retrieval date.

## Combined ratio

The P&C combined ratio improved to ~92.2–92.9 in 2025 (from ~96.6 in 2024) — the strongest underwriting performance in over a decade. Net underwriting gain ~$63B (vs ~$23B in 2024) (§3 #1).

## Catastrophe

Cat losses accounted for ~7.6 points of the 2025 combined ratio, down from ~8.8 in 2024 — strip cat to judge underwriting (§3 #4).

## By line

Homeowners posted an ~88.1 NCR (lowest in over a decade, even with Q1 LA wildfire activity); personal auto ~91.8; general liability and commercial auto remained above 100 — the mix tells the story (§3 #6).
