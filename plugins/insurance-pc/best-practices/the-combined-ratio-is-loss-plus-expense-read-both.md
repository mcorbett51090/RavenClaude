# The combined ratio is loss plus expense — read both

**Status:** Absolute rule. **Constitution:** §3, §4.

## Use when
Any p&c insurance deliverable where this question is in play.

## The rule
The P&C industry hit a decade-best ~92.2 combined in 2025 (from ~96.6 in 2024); under 100 is an underwriting profit. Read the loss ratio and the expense ratio separately — they have different fixes.

## Why
This is house opinion §3 #1. Getting it wrong is the most common way a p&c insurance analysis misleads the operator who acts on it.

## How to apply
- Apply it before reaching for a method.
- Make the relevant number carry a definition, window, and baseline (§3 #1).
- Cite the source + date for any external figure, or mark it `[unverified — training knowledge]`.
