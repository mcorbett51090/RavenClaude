# Isolate the catastrophe load

**Status:** Pattern. **Constitution:** §3, §4.

## Use when
Any p&c insurance deliverable where this question is in play.

## The rule
Cat losses ran ~7.6 points of the 2025 combined ratio; an all-in loss ratio that buries cat makes the attritional book look worse or better than it is — strip cat to judge underwriting.

## Why
This is house opinion §3 #4. Getting it wrong is the most common way a p&c insurance analysis misleads the operator who acts on it.

## How to apply
- Apply it before reaching for a method.
- Make the relevant number carry a definition, window, and baseline (§3 #1).
- Cite the source + date for any external figure, or mark it `[unverified — training knowledge]`.
