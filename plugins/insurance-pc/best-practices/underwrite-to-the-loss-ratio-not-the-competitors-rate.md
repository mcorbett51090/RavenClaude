# Underwrite to the loss ratio, not the competitor's rate

**Status:** Absolute rule. **Constitution:** §3, §4.

## Use when
Any p&c insurance deliverable where this question is in play.

## The rule
Rate adequacy is priced to expected loss plus expense plus profit load; matching a competitor's price into an inadequate rate is how you grow into a loss.

## Why
This is house opinion §3 #2. Getting it wrong is the most common way a p&c insurance analysis misleads the operator who acts on it.

## How to apply
- Apply it before reaching for a method.
- Make the relevant number carry a definition, window, and baseline (§3 #1).
- Cite the source + date for any external figure, or mark it `[unverified — training knowledge]`.
