# Separate frequency from severity

**Status:** Pattern. **Constitution:** §3, §4.

## Use when
Any p&c insurance deliverable where this question is in play.

## The rule
A loss-ratio move is a frequency story, a severity story, or both, and they have opposite responses — social inflation drives severity; risk selection drives frequency.

## Why
This is house opinion §3 #3. Getting it wrong is the most common way a p&c insurance analysis misleads the operator who acts on it.

## How to apply
- Apply it before reaching for a method.
- Make the relevant number carry a definition, window, and baseline (§3 #1).
- Cite the source + date for any external figure, or mark it `[unverified — training knowledge]`.
