# Skill: Separate frequency from severity

Frequency and severity look like one number and need opposite fixes.

## Step 1 — Split the loss ratio
Claim count (frequency) vs cost per claim (severity) (§3 #3).

## Step 2 — Attribute severity
Social inflation, mix shift, or reserve strengthening.

## Step 3 — Attribute frequency
Risk selection, exposure growth, or external trend.

## Step 4 — Match the response
Risk-selection fix for frequency; pricing/claims fix for severity.

## Output
A frequency/severity split, an attribution of each, and the matched response.
