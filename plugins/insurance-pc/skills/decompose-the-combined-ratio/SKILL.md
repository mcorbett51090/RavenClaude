# Skill: Decompose the combined ratio

The combined ratio's two (then four) parts each have different fixes.

## Step 1 — Split loss vs expense
Loss ratio and expense ratio separately; under 100 combined is underwriting profit (§3 #1).

## Step 2 — Strip catastrophe
Separate cat load (~7.6 pts in 2025) from the attritional loss ratio (§3 #4).

## Step 3 — Attribute to line of business
Decompose by line — the mix, not the average, tells the story (§3 #6).

## Step 4 — Locate the driver
Name whether the move is expense, attritional loss, cat, or mix before prescribing.

## Output
A loss/expense, attritional/cat, by-line decomposition with the driver located.
