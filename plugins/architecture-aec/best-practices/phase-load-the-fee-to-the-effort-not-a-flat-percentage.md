# Phase-load the fee to the effort, not a flat percentage

**Status:** Absolute rule. **Constitution:** §3, §4.

## Use when
Any architecture & aec deliverable where this question is in play.

## The rule
Effort isn't even across schematic design, design development, construction documents, and construction administration; a fee that doesn't match the phase effort curve underwater-funds the heavy phases. [unverified — training knowledge]

## Why
This is house opinion §3 #1. Getting it wrong is the most common way a architecture & aec analysis misleads the operator who acts on it.

## How to apply
- Apply it before reaching for a method.
- Make the relevant number carry a definition, window, and baseline (§3 #1).
- Cite the source + date for any external figure, or mark it `[unverified — training knowledge]`.
