# Scope creep is the margin killer — control additional services

**Status:** Absolute rule. **Constitution:** §3, §4.

## Use when
Any architecture & aec deliverable where this question is in play.

## The rule
Unbilled scope changes and 'just one more option' erode the fee silently; additional-services authorization is how a firm protects margin, not a relationship risk to avoid.

## Why
This is house opinion §3 #2. Getting it wrong is the most common way a architecture & aec analysis misleads the operator who acts on it.

## How to apply
- Apply it before reaching for a method.
- Make the relevant number carry a definition, window, and baseline (§3 #1).
- Cite the source + date for any external figure, or mark it `[unverified — training knowledge]`.
