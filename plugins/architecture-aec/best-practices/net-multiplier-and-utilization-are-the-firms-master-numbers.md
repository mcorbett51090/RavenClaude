# Net multiplier and utilization are the firm's master numbers

**Status:** Pattern. **Constitution:** §3, §4.

## Use when
Any architecture & aec deliverable where this question is in play.

## The rule
The firm runs on billable utilization and net multiplier (net revenue ÷ direct labor); a busy firm with low utilization or multiplier is losing money on talent.

## Why
This is house opinion §3 #4. Getting it wrong is the most common way a architecture & aec analysis misleads the operator who acts on it.

## How to apply
- Apply it before reaching for a method.
- Make the relevant number carry a definition, window, and baseline (§3 #1).
- Cite the source + date for any external figure, or mark it `[unverified — training knowledge]`.
