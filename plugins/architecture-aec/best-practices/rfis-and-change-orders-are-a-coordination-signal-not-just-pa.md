# RFIs and change orders are a coordination signal, not just paperwork

**Status:** Pattern. **Constitution:** §3, §4.

## Use when
Any architecture & aec deliverable where this question is in play.

## The rule
A high RFI/change-order rate usually traces to document coordination quality; reading the pattern improves the next set, not just this project.

## Why
This is house opinion §3 #3. Getting it wrong is the most common way a architecture & aec analysis misleads the operator who acts on it.

## How to apply
- Apply it before reaching for a method.
- Make the relevant number carry a definition, window, and baseline (§3 #1).
- Cite the source + date for any external figure, or mark it `[unverified — training knowledge]`.
