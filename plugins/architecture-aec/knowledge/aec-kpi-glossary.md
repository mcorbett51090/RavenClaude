# Architecture/AEC KPI glossary

The metrics an architecture firm is judged on — formulas, the misreads, and where a figure needs a `[unverified]` mark.

## Practice metrics

Utilization (billable ÷ available), net multiplier (net revenue ÷ direct labor), overhead rate. The master firm numbers (§3 #4).

## Project metrics

Phase-loaded fee, hours-vs-fee by phase, additional-services capture, effort variance (§3 #1, #2).

## Document metrics

RFI/change-order rate, coordination-conflict count, constructability issues, CA cost (§3 #3, #5).

## Sourcing note

Figures in this file are from the author's domain knowledge and are marked `[unverified — training knowledge]` or `[ESTIMATE]` at point of use. Validate against a primary source before putting any figure in a client deliverable (§3 cite-or-mark rule).
