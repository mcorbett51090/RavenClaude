## Steps (traverse top-to-bottom; do not skip)
1. Compute utilization — Billable hours ÷ available hours, by staff (§3 #4).
2. Compute net multiplier — Net revenue ÷ direct labor.
3. Find the gap — Low utilization (capacity) vs low multiplier (pricing/effort).
4. Act — Map the fix to the gap — fee structure, scope, or staffing.
