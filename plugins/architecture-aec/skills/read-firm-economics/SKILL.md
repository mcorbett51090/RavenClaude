# Skill: Read firm economics

A busy firm with low utilization or multiplier loses money on talent.

## Step 1 — Compute utilization
Billable hours ÷ available hours, by staff (§3 #4).

## Step 2 — Compute net multiplier
Net revenue ÷ direct labor.

## Step 3 — Find the gap
Low utilization (capacity) vs low multiplier (pricing/effort).

## Step 4 — Act
Map the fix to the gap — fee structure, scope, or staffing.

## Output
A utilization and net-multiplier read with the gap identified and a matched fix.
