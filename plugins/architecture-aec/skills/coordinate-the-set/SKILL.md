# Skill: Coordinate the drawing set

Coordination is the document's real quality.

## Step 1 — Cross-check disciplines
Architectural vs structural/MEP conflicts (§3 #5).

## Step 2 — Read constructability
Flag details that are hard or costly to build.

## Step 3 — Tie to RFI risk
Predict where RFIs/change orders will come from (§3 #3).

## Step 4 — Stay decision-support
Present for the architect of record to resolve.

## Output
A coordination read with conflicts, constructability flags, and RFI-risk predictions, as decision-support.
