# Vacancy is the most expensive thing in the portfolio

Every day a unit sits vacant is revenue you never recover. Time-to-lease, turn time, and renewal rate are first-class metrics, and the unit-turn clock starts the day notice is given — not the day the unit empties. A renewal decision weighs the cost of turn plus vacancy loss against the rent upside, because retaining a good paying tenant usually beats the gap. Optimize the funnel and the turn against the vacancy-loss number, not against turn-cost in isolation.
