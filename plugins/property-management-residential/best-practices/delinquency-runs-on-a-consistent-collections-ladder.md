# Delinquency runs on one consistent, documented collections ladder

The same dated sequence — reminder → late notice → pay-or-quit → counsel — is applied to every delinquent account, never selectively. "We let the good tenant slide" is exactly the selective enforcement that creates both a fair-housing exposure and a financial-control gap. Document the ladder, apply it uniformly, and record each step with its date; the legal steps (pay-or-quit, eviction filing) are flagged to counsel, not executed as if the legality were settled.
