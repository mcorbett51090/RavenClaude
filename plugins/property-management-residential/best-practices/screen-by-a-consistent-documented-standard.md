# Screen by a consistent, documented standard applied to every applicant

The defense against a discrimination claim is the same written criteria — income multiple, credit/eviction history, occupancy standard — applied identically to every applicant, never ad-hoc per-applicant judgment. Document the standard before screening anyone against it, and record which criterion every denial rests on. A denial you can't tie to a written, uniformly-applied criterion is a fair-housing risk regardless of intent. (Criminal-history and source-of-income use is legally constrained — flag, don't freelance.)
