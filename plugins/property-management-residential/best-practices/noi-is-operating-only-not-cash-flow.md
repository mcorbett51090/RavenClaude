# NOI is operating only — never confuse it with cash flow

NOI = operating income − operating expenses, *excluding* debt service, capex, and depreciation. An owner statement that mixes in debt service is answering a different question (levered cash flow) — say which question you're answering. Capex (a roof, a renovation-grade turn) is not an operating expense; keep it out of NOI. The owner statement reports operations; the trust-account reconciliation, GL posting, and tax treatment are the books of record and belong to `finance`.
