# Habitability is non-negotiable and time-sensitive

No heat in winter, no water, no power, sewage backup, a gas leak, and no working lock are habitability/emergency events with a duty to act fast — they never sit in the routine work-order queue. When in doubt, treat the condition as an emergency and escalate; under-reacting to a gas smell or a no-heat call is the unrecoverable error. Whether the condition legally breaches the warranty of habitability is a counsel question; the operational duty to act fast does not wait on that answer.
