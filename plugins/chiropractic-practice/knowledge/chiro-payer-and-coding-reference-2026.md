## The active-vs-maintenance rule (stable concept)

- **Active/corrective care** — a documented condition, a functional deficit, and *expected improvement*; may be covered.
- **Supportive/maintenance care** — the patient has plateaued (no further functional improvement expected); continued care is generally **not** a covered benefit → cash / wellness plan, ABN where required.
- The **re-exam** is the mechanism that documents you're still on the active side; the **plateau** is the trigger to transition.

## Operating benchmarks (directional only — measure your own)

- **Patient Visit Average (PVA)** and **plan-completion rate** are the retention/health metrics that matter more than new-patient count `[verify-at-use]`.
- **Time-of-service collection** (verify benefits → quote responsibility → collect at visit) is the front-desk revenue-cycle discipline.
