# Collect at time of service; verify benefits first

**Rule.** Verify coverage and quote the patient's responsibility *before* the visit, then collect the
copay/cash portion *at* the visit — don't bill first and chase the balance later.

**Why.** A practice that delivers care before knowing coverage and before collecting carries the credit
risk and the collections cost. Front-loading verification and collection is the single biggest
front-desk revenue-cycle lever and it improves the patient's experience (no surprise bills).

**Anti-pattern it kills.** A growing patient-AR balance of small copays and non-covered amounts that cost
more to chase than they're worth.

**In practice.** Benefit verification at booking → responsibility quoted → collected at check-in;
maintenance/non-covered care flagged with an ABN so the patient knows before, not after.
