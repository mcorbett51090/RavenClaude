# No silent regulated sign-off

A CAPA closure, a deviation disposition, a use-as-is or rework decision on safety-critical product, and a recall call are accountable-human decisions — never auto-closed by an agent. This plugin *drafts* the disposition (the root-cause record, the corrective/preventive action, the effectiveness check, the control-plan update) and *escalates* it for sign-off; it does not sign. The standard behind the record (ISO 9001, IATF 16949, AS9100, or an FDA quality system) names who owns the signature and what evidence the closure requires. Surface that owner and that evidence; stop at the line where a human must accept the risk. A regulated record closed without the accountable signature is a finding waiting for the next audit — and on safety-critical product, a liability, not a shortcut.
