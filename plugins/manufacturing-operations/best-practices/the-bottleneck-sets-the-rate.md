# The bottleneck sets the rate

Per Theory of Constraints, plant throughput is governed by the single binding constraint. An hour lost at the bottleneck is an hour lost for the whole plant; an hour saved at a non-bottleneck is a mirage that just grows WIP. Find the constraint first (follow the pile of work-in-process), then identify → exploit → subordinate → elevate it. Optimizing a resource that isn't the constraint and calling it a throughput win is the most common — and most expensive — shop-floor error.
