# OEE denominators must be defined

OEE is Availability × Performance × Quality — and every factor has a denominator that can be gamed. Performance needs a *stated* ideal cycle time (a sandbagged ideal inflates the number); availability needs a *stated* split of planned vs unplanned downtime (reclassifying breakdowns as 'planned maintenance' flatters it). An OEE figure with undefined denominators is uncomparable theater. State the ideal cycle time and the downtime definitions, or don't quote the number.
