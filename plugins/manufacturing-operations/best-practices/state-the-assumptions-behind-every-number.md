# State the assumptions behind every number

Every plan, OEE figure, and quality disposition rests on assumptions — the forecast horizon and planning bucket, the ideal cycle time and downtime definitions, the SPC sample size and subgroup, the inspection AQL, the BOM effectivity. Surface them, don't bury them. A Cpk with no stability check or sample-size basis, an OEE with no ideal cycle time, a schedule with no capacity basis — each is the next argument on the floor. The unstated assumption is where the disagreement hides.
