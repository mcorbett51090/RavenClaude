All notable changes to this plugin are documented here. Versioning is semver; bump on every user-visible change (AGENTS.md).

## [0.1.0] — 2026-06-08

Initial release.

- **4 agents** — `hotel-operations-lead`, `revenue-management-analyst`, `labor-productivity-specialist`, `guest-experience-specialist`, each carrying the full scenario-authoring schema.
- **5 skills + 5 commands** — `read-revpar`, `compare-channels`, `read-booking-pace`, `size-labor`, `link-experience-revenue`.
- **4-file knowledge bank** — KPI glossary, unit economics, 2025–2026 context, and Mermaid decision trees.
- **`scripts/hotel_hospitality_operations_calc.py`** — stdlib calculator: `revpar`, `channel-cost`, `labor`. Decision-support only.
- **4 templates · 8 best-practice rules · scenarios bank · 1 advisory hook**.
