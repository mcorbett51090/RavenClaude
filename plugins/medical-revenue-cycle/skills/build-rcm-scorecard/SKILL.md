# Skill: Build an RCM scorecard

The scorecard makes the cash cycle visible weekly.

## Step 1 — Lead with net collection rate
Defined against allowed, with window and baseline (§3 #4).

## Step 2 — Add the efficiency metrics
First-pass resolution and clean-claim rate (§3 #2).

## Step 3 — Add denial analytics
Denial rate by category and payer (§3 #5).

## Step 4 — Add the cash-cycle metric
Days-in-A/R by aging bucket (§3 #3).

## Output
A net-collection-led RCM scorecard with efficiency, denial, and A/R metrics, each defined and baselined.
