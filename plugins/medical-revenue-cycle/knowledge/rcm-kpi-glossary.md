# RCM KPI glossary

The metrics a revenue cycle is judged on — formulas, the misreads, and where a benchmark needs a date.

## Efficiency metrics

First-pass resolution (~90% benchmark), clean-claim rate (95–98%), denial rate (target <5%). First-pass is the master efficiency number (§3 #2).

## Cash metrics

Net collection rate (95–98%+), days-in-A/R (<30 high-performing), A/R aging buckets. Net, not gross, measures the cycle (§3 #4).

## Denial metrics

Denial rate by category (eligibility/auth/coding/documentation) and payer, appeal overturn rate, write-off rate (§3 #5).
