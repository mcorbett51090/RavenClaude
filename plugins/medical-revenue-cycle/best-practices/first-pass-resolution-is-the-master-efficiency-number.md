# First-pass resolution is the master efficiency number

**Status:** Absolute rule. **Constitution:** §3, §4.

## Use when
Any medical revenue cycle deliverable where this question is in play.

## The rule
First-pass resolution (claims paid without rework) benchmarks ~90%, with high performers pushing clean-claim rates to 95–98%. Every point of rework is cost and delayed cash.

## Why
This is house opinion §3 #2. Getting it wrong is the most common way a medical revenue cycle analysis misleads the operator who acts on it.

## How to apply
- Apply it before reaching for a method.
- Make the relevant number carry a definition, window, and baseline (§3 #1).
- Cite the source + date for any external figure, or mark it `[unverified — training knowledge]`.
