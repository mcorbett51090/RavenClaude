# Days in A/R is the cash-cycle headline

**Status:** Pattern. **Constitution:** §3, §4.

## Use when
Any medical revenue cycle deliverable where this question is in play.

## The rule
High performers run days-in-A/R under 30 (31–40 acceptable per MGMA/AAFP). Read A/R by aging bucket and payer, not as a single average.

## Why
This is house opinion §3 #3. Getting it wrong is the most common way a medical revenue cycle analysis misleads the operator who acts on it.

## How to apply
- Apply it before reaching for a method.
- Make the relevant number carry a definition, window, and baseline (§3 #1).
- Cite the source + date for any external figure, or mark it `[unverified — training knowledge]`.
