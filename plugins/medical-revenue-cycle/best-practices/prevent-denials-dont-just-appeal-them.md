# Prevent denials; don't just appeal them

**Status:** Absolute rule. **Constitution:** §3, §4.

## Use when
Any medical revenue cycle deliverable where this question is in play.

## The rule
Initial denials reached ~11.8% in 2024 and trend toward 12–15% in 2025 — best-in-class is <5% (world-class <3%). The cheapest denial is the one that never happens; work root cause, not just the appeal queue.

## Why
This is house opinion §3 #1. Getting it wrong is the most common way a medical revenue cycle analysis misleads the operator who acts on it.

## How to apply
- Apply it before reaching for a method.
- Make the relevant number carry a definition, window, and baseline (§3 #1).
- Cite the source + date for any external figure, or mark it `[unverified — training knowledge]`.
