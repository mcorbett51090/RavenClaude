# Stitch identity across the login boundary

Tie a user's anonymous pre-login activity to their known identity once they identify, so funnels, attribution, and retention analyses survive the signup boundary. Broken identity stitching silently severs every cross-session and cross-device analysis — the acquisition that led to a signup looks like two unrelated users. Get the anonymous-to-known mapping right at instrumentation time, because it cannot be reconstructed later.
