# Build the apparatus; route significance to applied-statistics

This team owns assignment, exposure logging, flags, and instrumentation — the machinery that produces clean experimental data. Whether a result is statistically significant, what the required sample size is, and whether a lift is real rather than noise are applied-statistics' calls. Computing a p-value here, or declaring a winner from the dashboard, bypasses the discipline that keeps experimentation honest. Produce trustworthy data and hand the verdict over.
