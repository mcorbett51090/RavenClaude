# LTV:CAC is the master ratio — 3:1 is the line

**Status:** Absolute rule. **Constitution:** §3, §4.

## Use when
Any e-commerce & dtc deliverable where this question is in play.

## The rule
A 3:1 LTV:CAC is the minimum for sustainability; below 2:1 is an immediate problem. CAC alone (avg DTC ~$45–$70, up ~40% in two years) is half the story — read it against lifetime value.

## Why
This is house opinion §3 #1. Getting it wrong is the most common way a e-commerce & dtc analysis misleads the operator who acts on it.

## How to apply
- Apply it before reaching for a method.
- Make the relevant number carry a definition, window, and baseline (§3 #1).
- Cite the source + date for any external figure, or mark it `[unverified — training knowledge]`.
