# Retention is the profit engine — the second purchase is everything

**Status:** Pattern. **Constitution:** §3, §4.

## Use when
Any e-commerce & dtc deliverable where this question is in play.

## The rule
The average DTC brand retains just ~28.2% for a second purchase, yet ~60% of revenue comes from returning customers; the repeat rate, not the acquisition rate, compounds.

## Why
This is house opinion §3 #3. Getting it wrong is the most common way a e-commerce & dtc analysis misleads the operator who acts on it.

## How to apply
- Apply it before reaching for a method.
- Make the relevant number carry a definition, window, and baseline (§3 #1).
- Cite the source + date for any external figure, or mark it `[unverified — training knowledge]`.
