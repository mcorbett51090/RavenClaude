# Contribution margin, not revenue, is the scoreboard

**Status:** Absolute rule. **Constitution:** §3, §4.

## Use when
Any e-commerce & dtc deliverable where this question is in play.

## The rule
Revenue net of COGS, CAC, shipping, and returns is what funds the business; a brand can grow revenue while contribution margin goes negative.

## Why
This is house opinion §3 #2. Getting it wrong is the most common way a e-commerce & dtc analysis misleads the operator who acts on it.

## How to apply
- Apply it before reaching for a method.
- Make the relevant number carry a definition, window, and baseline (§3 #1).
- Cite the source + date for any external figure, or mark it `[unverified — training knowledge]`.
