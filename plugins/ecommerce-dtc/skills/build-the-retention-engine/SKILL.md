# Skill: Build the retention engine

The average brand keeps ~28% for a second purchase; retention is the profit engine.

## Step 1 — Measure cohort retention
Repeat-purchase rate and cohort curves (§3 #3).

## Step 2 — Find the second-purchase gap
Where and why first-time buyers don't return.

## Step 3 — Build the levers
Post-purchase flow, subscription, replenishment, loyalty.

## Step 4 — Re-measure LTV
Track the repeat rate and its LTV impact.

## Output
A cohort-retention read, the second-purchase gap, retention levers, and the LTV impact.
