# DTC benchmarks (2025–2026)

Dated benchmarks for a unit-economics read. Acquisition costs rose sharply — every figure carries a source and a retrieval date.

## Acquisition

Average DTC CAC ~$45–$70 (some sources ~$68–$84), up ~40% in two years and ~24.7% YoY in 2025. CAC varies widely by vertical (pet ~$23, fashion ~$37, beauty ~$42, supplements ~$89) (§3 #5).

## Conversion

Average stores convert ~1.4–1.8%; a good benchmark is ~2.5–3%; the top ~10% convert above ~4.7% (§3 #4).

## Retention & ratio

~60% of DTC revenue comes from returning customers, yet the average brand retains just ~28.2% for a second purchase. A 3:1 LTV:CAC is the sustainability minimum; below 2:1 is an immediate problem (§3 #1, #3).
