# E-commerce/DTC KPI glossary

The metrics a DTC brand is judged on — formulas, the misreads, and where a benchmark needs a date.

## Unit-economics metrics

CAC (by channel), LTV (by cohort), LTV:CAC (3:1 line), contribution margin, AOV. The ratio and margin are the master numbers (§3 #1, #2).

## Funnel metrics

Conversion rate (avg ~1.4–1.8%), add-to-cart rate, checkout abandonment, sessions, traffic quality (§3 #4).

## Retention metrics

Repeat-purchase rate (~28% second purchase), cohort retention, purchase frequency, return rate (§3 #3, #6).
