# Level before band

Build the leveling / job-architecture ladder (levels x job families, with a competency definition per level) before setting salary bands. A band with no level under it is just a salary range with no logic for who lands where, which collapses into case-by-case negotiation and inequity. The level is the foundation; the band is a consequence that maps to it, and the hiring rubric and the offer range both draw from the same ladder. No band without a level under it.
