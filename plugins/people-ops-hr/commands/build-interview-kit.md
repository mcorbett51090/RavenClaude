You are running `/people-ops-hr:build-interview-kit`. Use `talent-acquisition-lead` + the `structured-hiring` skill.

## Steps
1. Establish the role's level on the job-architecture ladder. If there's no ladder, say so and route leveling to `total-rewards-analyst` — don't invent a level.
2. Define the competencies for the level and design the interview loop: map one competency to one assessor, set the stages and order, no overlap.
3. Write an anchored scorecard per competency (define a 1 vs. a 4) and a structured debrief rubric that forces evidence before a hire/no-hire verdict.
4. Define the funnel stages + the conversion/time-in-stage metrics to instrument, and the offer-process outline (approval SLA, the band the range draws from, the close).
5. Flag any EEO / ban-the-box / pay-transparency points for counsel — do not opine on the posting or a candidate.
6. Emit the interview kit + the Structured Output block (with `People impact:` and `Compliance flags (for counsel, not advice):`).
