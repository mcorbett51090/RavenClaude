# Ball-in-court is the unit of progress

Every RFI, submittal, change, and punch item has exactly one party who owes the next action and a date it's due. "In review" with no owner and no date is a stall, not a status. A log without ball-in-court can't show who's late, so nothing gets escalated until it's already blocking the field. Make the owner and the due date a required field on every open item.
