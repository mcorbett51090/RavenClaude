# Safety is planned per task; OSHA is the floor

A job-hazard analysis breaks the task into steps and names the hazard and the specific control for each — fall protection at height, exclusion zones, rigging, LOTO, shoring. The toolbox talk matches the day's actual high-risk work, delivered to the crew doing it, with a sign-in. Cite the applicable OSHA 29 CFR 1926 requirement as the floor, but the control is the one this task needs — a generic talk on a confined-space day is safety theater.
