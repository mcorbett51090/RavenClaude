# Schedule submittals backward from the install date

A submittal's required-by date = need-by (install) − lead time − review time. Compute it when the submittal register is built, not the week the material is needed. If that math puts the required-by date in the past, the item is already late — surface it as a schedule risk now and expedite review or re-sequence. Tracking a submittal with no lead-time-aware required-by date guarantees the block surfaces at the worst possible moment.
