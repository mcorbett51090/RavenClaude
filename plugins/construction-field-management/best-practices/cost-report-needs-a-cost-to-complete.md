# The cost report is only honest with a cost-to-complete

Budget-vs-actual reported as actuals against budget is a mirage. The honest number is committed vs. actual vs. forecast by cost code, with a real cost-to-complete and a projected final cost. "Under budget" while changes are unposted and signed subcontracts/POs are unbilled hides an overrun. Unbilled commitments are real money — leave them out and the cost report lies the month before the overrun lands.
