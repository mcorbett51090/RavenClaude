# Every number is assurable or it's a liability

A disclosed figure with no traceable activity data, emission factor, and calculation method is a finding waiting to happen. Design the evidence trail *into* the number — activity data, factor (with source and vintage), method, and preparer/reviewer sign-off authored alongside the figure, not retrofitted after disclosure. The assurance level (limited vs reasonable) sets how deep the trail must go; know it from the start so the evidence is built to the right bar rather than reconstructed under audit pressure.
