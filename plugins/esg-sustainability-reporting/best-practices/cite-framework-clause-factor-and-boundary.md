# Cite the framework clause, the factor source, and the boundary — always

Each material disclosure names the specific requirement it satisfies (ESRS / IFRS S1·S2 / GRI / SEC). Each emission figure names the activity data, the emission factor with its source and vintage, and the consolidation boundary it was calculated under. An unmapped disclosure is either out of scope or under-justified; a factor with no vintage is unusable because nobody can tell whether it's current. Citations are not decoration — they are what lets an assurer trace, and a reader trust, every number and claim.
