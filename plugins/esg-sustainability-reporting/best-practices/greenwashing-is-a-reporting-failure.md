# Greenwashing is a reporting failure

No unsubstantiated claim, no cherry-picked boundary, no offset counted as a reduction. Every sustainability claim ("carbon neutral", "net zero aligned", "X% reduction") needs a substantiation reference or it is removed, not softened. Report gross emissions and offsets separately — a "reduction" that is actually an offset purchase is a misstatement, not a nuance. A claim the evidence can't carry is a liability under both assurance and anti-greenwashing regulation; delete it or rewrite it to what the evidence supports.
