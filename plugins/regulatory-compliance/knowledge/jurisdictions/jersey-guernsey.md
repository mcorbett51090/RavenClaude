# Channel Islands — Jersey (JFSC) & Guernsey (GFSC)

> **Last reviewed:** 2026-06-04. Primary-source map of the **Jersey Financial Services Commission (JFSC)** and **Guernsey Financial Services Commission (GFSC)** for the `channel-islands-specialist`. **Refresh when** (a) a sectoral law is restated/consolidated; (b) a MONEYVAL evaluation changes status; (c) any `[unverified]` marker is resolved against the primary law.
>
> **Sourcing caveat:** some JFSC pages returned **HTTP 403 to direct fetch** (the bank count and the deposit-taking parent-policy wording in particular); those are marked `[unverified]`. Statute titles/numbers are confirmed against jerseylaw.je and guernseylegalresources.gg.

Both are **Crown Dependencies** evaluated by **MONEYVAL** (not CFATF). Both punch above their weight in funds and fiduciary work. **Two naming traps below — read the callouts.**

---

## A) JERSEY — JFSC

### A1. The regulator
- **Financial Services Commission (Jersey) Law 1998** (`l_11_1998`) establishes the JFSC.
- **⚠ Naming trap:** the **Commission Law** (`l_11_1998`, the regulator) is *distinct* from the **Financial Services (Jersey) Law 1998** (`l_32_1998`, the licensing law in A3). **Two separate 1998 laws.**
- The JFSC is also the **Registrar of Companies** for Jersey. Risk-based model with sector Codes of Practice.

### A2. Banking
- **Banking Business (Jersey) Law 1991** — registration regime for deposit-taking in/from Jersey; Code of Practice issued under **Article 19A**.
- ~**19 licensed deposit-takers** as at 30 Jun 2025 `[unverified — JFSC page 403'd]`.
- Historic **"top 500 banks by capital" parental-group policy** relaxed to an expectation of **global Top 1000**, applied flexibly `[unverified exact current wording]`.

### A3. Investment / fund services / trust company business — the single licensing law
- **Financial Services (Jersey) Law 1998** (`l_32_1998`) — one licensing law covering **five classes** of "financial service business": **Investment Business, Trust Company Business (TCB), Fund Services Business, General Insurance Mediation Business, Money Service Business** (+ AIF Services Business). Registration under **Article 9**; class definitions in **Article 2**. TCB under **Article 2(3)**; Class G covers natural-person directors. Each class has its own JFSC Code of Practice.

### A4. Funds
- **Collective Investment Funds (Jersey) Law 1988** — the CIF (certified funds) regime.
- **Jersey Private Fund (JPF)** — governed by the JFSC JPF Guide; **not a CIF**; max **50 investors/offers**; each investor "professional"/"eligible" **or** investing **≥ £250,000**.
- **Expert Funds** and **Listed Funds** — CIFs under the 1988 Law, lighter-touch. **Eligible Investor Funds** ("Notification Only") — investors commit **≥ US$1m**.

### A5. Insurance (brief)
- **Insurance Business (Jersey) Law 1996** — permit regime. **Category A** = insurers already authorised/supervised outside Jersey; **Category B** = other cases (typically Jersey-incorporated).

### A6. AML / CFT / CPF
- **Proceeds of Crime (Jersey) Law 1999** (principal AML law); **Money Laundering (Jersey) Order 2008** (operational); **Proceeds of Crime (Supervisory Bodies) (Jersey) Law 2008**.
- **JFSC AML/CFT/CPF Handbook** — current edition effective **1 Sep 2023**; recast Schedule 2 activities effective 30 Jan 2023.
- **FIU**: Jersey's Financial Intelligence Unit.
- **MONEYVAL 5th-round MER (publ. 24 Jul 2024):** Compliant/Largely Compliant on **39 of 40** Recommendations; **High/Substantial on 7 of 11** Immediate Outcomes — among the top-tier results globally.

### A7. Beneficial ownership
- **Financial Services (Disclosure and Provision of Information) (Jersey) Law 2020** — central register of beneficial owners & significant persons; in force **6 Jan 2021** (addresses FATF Recommendation 24). BO data not public.

## B) GUERNSEY — GFSC

**Context:** Guernsey ran a "revision of regulatory laws project" — the four core **sectoral** laws were restated/consolidated and **largely came into force 1 Nov 2021**, with enforcement powers centralised in the **Financial Services Business (Enforcement Powers) (Bailiwick of Guernsey) Law 2020**. **⚠ The 1987 FSC Law remains the constitution** — what changed in 2020/2021 is the restatement of the *sectoral* laws, not a re-enactment of the 1987 constitutional law.

### B1. The regulator
- **Financial Services Commission (Bailiwick of Guernsey) Law 1987** (consolidated text) constitutes the GFSC. Enforcement/sanctions/penalties moved to the 2020 Enforcement Powers Law.

### B2. Banking
- **Banking Supervision (Bailiwick of Guernsey) Law 2020** — GFSC is licensing authority; minimum licensing criteria; States depositor compensation scheme.

### B3. Investment / funds
- **Protection of Investors (Bailiwick of Guernsey) Law 2020** ("POI Law") — restated investment-business + fund licensing; **Registered** and **Authorised** collective investment scheme regimes.
- **Private Investment Fund (PIF)** — governed by the PIF Rules and Guidance 2021; open- or closed-ended; GFSC declares **registered within one business day** of a complete application via a licensed administrator's representation.

### B4. Fiduciary (trust & corporate services)
- **Regulation of Fiduciaries, Administration Businesses and Company Directors, etc (Bailiwick of Guernsey) Law 2020** ("Fiduciaries Law") — in force **1 Nov 2021**, replacing the 2000 Law. Regulated activities: trustee, enforcer/protector, company director, partner, executor/estate administrator, foundation official. Licence tiers: **primary / secondary / personal** fiduciary licence. PTCs need a full licence or "limited permission."

### B5. Insurance (brief — captive hub)
- **Insurance Business (Bailiwick of Guernsey) Law 2002** — insurers split into **international** (non-local risk; pure captive or commercial) vs **domestic**; PCC/ICC cell structures available.

### B6. AML / CFT / CPF
- **Criminal Justice (Proceeds of Crime) (Bailiwick of Guernsey) Law 1999** — all Bailiwick-indictable offences are predicate offences (s.1(1)); **Schedule 3** imposes AML/CFT obligations on specified businesses.
- **GFSC Handbook on Countering Financial Crime (AML/CFT/CPF)** — rules + guidance under Schedule 3.
- **FIU**: the **Guernsey Financial Intelligence Service (FIS)**.
- **MONEYVAL 5th-round MER** (on-site 15–26 Apr 2024, publ. 2025): **passed all 40** Recommendations on technical compliance; **pass on 6 of 11** Immediate Outcomes; placed in **regular follow-up**.

### B7. Beneficial ownership
- **Beneficial Ownership of Legal Persons (Guernsey) Law 2017** — in force **15 Aug 2017**; establishes the **Office of the Registrar of Beneficial Ownership**; register **not public** (designated persons only; physically held within FIS).

## Channel Islands licence-class / regulator map

| Sector | Jersey — governing law | Guernsey — governing law |
|---|---|---|
| Regulator constitution | FSC (Jersey) Law **1998** | FSC (BoG) Law **1987** |
| Banking | Banking Business (Jersey) Law **1991** | Banking Supervision (BoG) Law **2020** |
| Investment business | Financial Services (Jersey) Law **1998** (class) | Protection of Investors (BoG) Law **2020** |
| Funds (CIS) | Collective Investment Funds (Jersey) Law **1988** | Protection of Investors (BoG) Law **2020** |
| Light-touch private fund | Jersey Private Fund (JPF) — JFSC Guide | Private Investment Fund (PIF) — PIF Rules **2021** |
| Trust & corporate services | Financial Services (Jersey) Law **1998** (TCB class) | Fiduciaries Law **2020** |
| Insurance | Insurance Business (Jersey) Law **1996** | Insurance Business (BoG) Law **2002** |
| AML principal law | Proceeds of Crime (Jersey) Law **1999** | Criminal Justice (Proceeds of Crime) (BoG) Law **1999** |
| AML operational | Money Laundering (Jersey) Order **2008** + JFSC Handbook | Sch. 3 POC Law + GFSC Handbook |
| Beneficial ownership | FS (Disclosure & Provision of Information) (Jersey) Law **2020** | Beneficial Ownership of Legal Persons (Guernsey) Law **2017** |
