# BMA filing & fee calendar — cross-sector quick-reference

> **Last reviewed:** 2026-06-04. A consolidated calendar view of what each BMA non-insurance sector files and when — distilled from [`supervision-and-filings.md`](supervision-and-filings.md) (which carries the full context). Use this to scope deadlines; use the source file for the supervisory/enforcement detail. **Refresh when** a sector's return cadence, the annual-fee date, or a code-compliance deadline changes.
>
> **Sourcing caveat:** cadences are confirmed where [`supervision-and-filings.md`](supervision-and-filings.md) confirms them; exact **fee figures** and several **MSB/DAB/fund-admin cadences** are `[unverified]` (locked in the 403-blocked 2025 Fees PDF). Treat this as a planning aid, not a substitute for the current BMA filing instructions — always read those before a live submission.

## Adjacent (non-BMA) filings every Bermuda entity should track

*(Administered by the RoC / Office of the Tax Commissioner / Corporate Income Tax Agency, not the BMA — see [`economic-substance-and-tax.md`](economic-substance-and-tax.md).)*

- **Economic-substance declaration** (Registrar of Companies) — annual.
- **CRS / FATCA** reporting (Tax Information Reporting Portal) — annual.
- **CbCR** (Office of the Tax Commissioner) — for €750m+ groups.
- **Corporate Income Tax** registration/returns — for in-scope MNE Constituent Entities (FY from 1 Jan 2025).
