# Prioritize by evidence, not the loudest voice

Use a transparent prioritization framework — RICE, cost-of-delay, or opportunity scoring — so trade-offs are explicit and arguable rather than settled by whoever has the most authority or volume in the room. The point is not the precision of the score; it is forcing reach, impact, confidence, and effort into the open where they can be challenged with evidence. A framework with invented inputs is just the HiPPO holding a spreadsheet.
