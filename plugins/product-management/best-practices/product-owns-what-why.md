# Product owns what/why; project owns how/when

Keep product management in the problem, outcome, and prioritization lane and route delivery scheduling, scope/change control, RAID, and ceremonies to project-management. Conflating the two muddies both: the product roadmap becomes a dated Gantt of features with false precision, and delivery loses the outcome context that should drive trade-offs. Clear ownership of what-and-why versus how-and-when keeps each discipline sharp.
