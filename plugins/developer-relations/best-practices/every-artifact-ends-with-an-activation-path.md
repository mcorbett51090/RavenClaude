# Every artifact ends with an activation path

**Status:** Absolute rule.

**Rule:** Every piece of content — post, talk, demo, doc, community answer — ends with the next
concrete step toward value: a repo, a quickstart, a sandbox, a CLI command. Content that informs but
provides no path converts nothing.

## Why

This is the single most common gap between content that "does well" (impressions, applause) and
content that activates. A developer leaves a great talk or post genuinely interested — and then does
nothing, because there was no obvious next step while the interest was hot. The activation path
captures that intent at its peak. Without it, the content's entire audience leaks at the end.

## What it looks like in practice

- Each artifact ends with one clear, concrete next action toward the developer's own first success.
- The path is specific (a short URL to a runnable repo) not vague ("check out our docs").
- The path's conversion is measured (post → quickstart starts → activations) via `content_roi`.

## Anti-pattern

A polished blog post or talk that ends with "thanks for reading/watching" and a logo. The hook flags
content/program work with no journey stage; this rule adds that a content artifact with no next-step
activation path is incomplete regardless of its quality.
