# Track the notice window, not just the expiry

An auto-renew fires unless you give notice within a window before expiry — so the notice-window deadline, not the expiry date, is the actionable one. Track it per contract and alert in tiers (90/60/30 days) to a named owner, so a non-renewal or renegotiation decision is made while there is still time. A renewal alert that fires at expiry is a decision you didn't get to make; a missed notice window locks you into another term you didn't choose.
