# Signature is the start of the lifecycle, not the end

A signed contract is a list of commitments, not a closed task. Extract every obligation — deliverables, SLAs, payment terms, notice periods, audit rights — and make each one a tracked item with a named owner and a due date or trigger. An obligation with no owner is one nobody meets; an untracked one leaks after the ink dries. The manage→renew phases are where the value (and the exposure) of a contract actually plays out.
