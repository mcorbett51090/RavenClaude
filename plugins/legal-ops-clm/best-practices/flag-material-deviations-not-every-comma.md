# Flag material deviations, not every comma

A redline review surfaces the deviations from standard/fallback that change risk — and notes the rest without escalating. Escalating every comma drowns the signal and erodes trust in the review. Concentrate on the key clauses where exposure lives (limitation of liability, indemnity, IP ownership, term/termination, confidentiality). Every flag carries a risk tier (within fallback / beyond fallback / walk-away) and a named approver, or it is just a highlight nobody can act on.
