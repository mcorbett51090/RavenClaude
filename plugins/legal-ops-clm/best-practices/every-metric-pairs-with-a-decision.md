# Every metric pairs with a decision

Before reporting a legal-ops number, name the operational decision a bad value would trigger. Cycle time by request class, intake volume and backlog, self-serve rate, escalation/risk mix, renewals-at-risk — each tied to a staffing or process action. A metric with no paired decision is vanity: "tickets closed" and "emails answered" can rise while legal is still a bottleneck. Pair throughput signals with a quality/risk signal so speed doesn't hide a worsening risk posture.
