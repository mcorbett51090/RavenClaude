# Structured intake, triaged by risk — not a DM

Legal work enters through one structured front door that captures request type, risk, value, counterparty, and deadline — not Slack, email, and hallway. You cannot triage or track what you cannot see. Route by risk and value, not arrival order: a standard NDA and a bet-the-company MSA are different queues. Self-serve the low-risk majority and reserve scarce lawyer time for the consequential.
