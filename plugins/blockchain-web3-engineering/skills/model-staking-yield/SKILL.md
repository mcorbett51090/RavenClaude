# Skill: Model staking yield

A modeled yield is illustrative engineering, never a promised return — route investment questions to a licensed authority (§3 #8, §2).

## Step 1 — Take the inputs
Staked amount, gross annual reward rate, validator commission.

## Step 2 — Compute net APR
Net APR = gross rate × (1 − commission) via `blockchain_web3_calc.py staking-yield`.

## Step 3 — Mark it illustrative
Label the output not-financial-advice and date the volatile price/rate inputs (§3 #8).

## Step 4 — Stress the sustainability
Is the reward funded or diluting — incentive soundness, not a return promise (§3 #7).

## Output
An illustrative net APR + annual reward, marked not-financial-advice, with volatile inputs dated and routing noted.
