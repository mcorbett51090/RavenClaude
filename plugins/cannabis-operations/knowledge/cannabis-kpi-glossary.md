# Cannabis operations KPI glossary

The metrics a cannabis operation is judged on — formulas, the misreads, and where a number needs a state and a date.

## Compliance metrics

Track-and-trace reconciliation rate, inventory discrepancy rate, test-fail rate, audit findings. Reconciliation is the license (§3 #1).

## Retail metrics

Gross margin by category, basket size, units-per-transaction, inventory turns/days-on-hand, budtender productivity (§3 #4, #5).

## Financial metrics

After-280E effective tax rate, COGS-allocation ratio, contribution margin — every position is CPA decision-support (§3 #2).
