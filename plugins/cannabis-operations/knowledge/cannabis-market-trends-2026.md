# Cannabis market & rules (2025–2026)

Dated market and regulatory context. The market and rules move constantly — every figure carries a source and a retrieval date.

## Market size

The U.S. cannabis market was projected at ~$45.35B in 2025, growing ~11.5% annually through 2030; the U.S. accounts for ~54% of worldwide legal sales (§3 #8).

## 280E & scheduling

Section 280E disallows ordinary deductions, making COGS allocation essential; moving to Schedule III would remove 280E and is estimated to save a typical dispensary ~$268k/yr (industry-wide ~$1.6B–$2.2B in incremental after-tax cash) (§3 #2).

## Track-and-trace

Seed-to-sale tracking is mandatory but varies by state — Metrc, BioTrack, or LeafData — each with specific requirements; the Metrc rollout and tag costs have been a recurring operator pain point (§3 #1, #3).
