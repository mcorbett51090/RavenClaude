## 280E

COGS allocation is existential and is CPA decision-support (§3 #2).

> **UPDATE (2026-04-23, effective 2026-04-28) — 280E no longer applies to FDA-approved + state-licensed MEDICAL operators.** The DOJ/DEA downscheduled FDA-approved marijuana drugs and state-licensed MEDICAL marijuana from Schedule I to **Schedule III**, which removes §280E for those operators (major margin/tax relief — ordinary deductions become allowable) and creates a DEA-registration pathway. **RECREATIONAL / adult-use remains Schedule I, so 280E and its existential COGS-allocation discipline still bind adult-use operators in full.** A broader-rescheduling administrative hearing runs **2026-06-29 to 2026-07-15** (could extend relief to adult-use; outcome pending). Confirm the operator's segment (medical vs adult-use) before scoping 280E work. Source: [Federal Register — Rescheduling of Marijuana (2026-08177)](https://www.federalregister.gov/documents/2026/04/28/2026-08177/schedules-of-controlled-substances-rescheduling-of-marijuana) — retrieved 2026-06-25.
