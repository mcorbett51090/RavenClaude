# Seed-to-sale traceability is the license — reconcile it daily

**Status:** Absolute rule. **Constitution:** §3, §4.

## Use when
Any cannabis operations deliverable where this question is in play.

## The rule
Mandatory track-and-trace (Metrc, BioTrack, or LeafData depending on the state) is the condition of the license; a physical-vs-system inventory discrepancy is a compliance event, not a bookkeeping error.

## Why
This is house opinion §3 #1. Getting it wrong is the most common way a cannabis operations analysis misleads the operator who acts on it.

## How to apply
- Apply it before reaching for a method.
- Make the relevant number carry a definition, window, and baseline (§3 #1).
- Cite the source + date for any external figure, or mark it `[unverified — training knowledge]`.
