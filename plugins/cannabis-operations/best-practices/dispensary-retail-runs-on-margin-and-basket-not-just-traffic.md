# Dispensary retail runs on margin and basket, not just traffic

**Status:** Pattern. **Constitution:** §3, §4.

## Use when
Any cannabis operations deliverable where this question is in play.

## The rule
Gross margin by category, basket size, and units-per-transaction drive the store; discounting to chase footfall erodes the margin the 280E burden already squeezes.

## Why
This is house opinion §3 #4. Getting it wrong is the most common way a cannabis operations analysis misleads the operator who acts on it.

## How to apply
- Apply it before reaching for a method.
- Make the relevant number carry a definition, window, and baseline (§3 #1).
- Cite the source + date for any external figure, or mark it `[unverified — training knowledge]`.
