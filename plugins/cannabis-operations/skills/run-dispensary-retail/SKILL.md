# Skill: Run dispensary retail on margin

The store runs on margin and basket, not footfall.

## Step 1 — Read category margin
Gross margin by category against the 280E-squeezed target (§3 #4).

## Step 2 — Read basket and UPT
Basket size and units-per-transaction, by budtender.

## Step 3 — Read turns
Inventory turns by category, flagging aged/perishable flower (§3 #5).

## Step 4 — Lift the levers
Assortment, cross-sell, and budtender productivity over discounting.

## Output
A category-margin, basket/UPT, and turns read with the margin-lifting levers.
