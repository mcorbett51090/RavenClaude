`brand-strategy-and-naming` skill.

> Thin orchestration: this plugin owns strategy → identity → brand book, and DELEGATES tokens

> application (`web-design:visual-designer`). Not legal advice — IP/naming claims route to `security-reviewer`.
> Prices are `[unverified]` (aggregator calibration, not quotes).

## Steps

1. **Run discovery** — the questionnaire in `skills/brand-strategy-and-naming/SKILL.md` (business, the real
   alternative, audience, emotional job, admired/avoided brands, negative constraints, where the brand lives).
2. **Set the tier** by traversing the **tier-selection** tree in `knowledge/brand-decision-trees.md`:
   - **Starter** — logo + basic palette/type + mini guide (`[unverified]` calibration ~$2–7K).
   - **Mid** — full identity WITH strategy + voice + brand book (`[unverified]` ~$7–25K).
   - **Comprehensive** — mid + messaging + web token handoff + collateral (`[unverified]` ~$25–100K+).
   State the tier deliverables and mark every price `[unverified]`.
3. **Author the strategy brief** into `templates/brand-strategy-brief.md` — positioning, value prop, audience,
   archetype (rejected alternatives named). This artifact is the **strategy-before-visuals gate**: no concept
   generation until it exists.
4. **Declare scope** — what's deep (logo/color/type/tokens/brand-book/voice) vs direction (icon/imagery) vs
   out-of-v1 (motion, extensive stationery). Don't sell "deep everywhere."
5. **Emit** the strategy brief + the tier + the next step (`/generate-identity-concepts` once strategy is
   approved) + the Structured Output block. Flag any obvious naming/TM collision to `security-reviewer`.
