# Skill: Read live-ops vital signs

A game that doesn't retain can't monetize.

## Step 1 — Read retention
D1/D7/D30 retention and the drop-off point (§3 #4).

## Step 2 — Gate on retention
Only optimize monetization once retention holds.

## Step 3 — Read monetization
ARPDAU, conversion, and the economy health.

## Step 4 — Plan the cadence
Content/events roadmap to sustain engagement (§3 #7).

## Output
A retention read, a retention-gated monetization read, and a live-service cadence.
