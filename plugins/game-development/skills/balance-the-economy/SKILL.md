# Skill: Balance the game economy

An economy balanced by intuition breaks retention.

## Step 1 — Map sources and sinks
Every way currency enters and leaves (§3 #5).

## Step 2 — Set the pacing
Progression pace against session length and retention.

## Step 3 — Find the imbalance
Inflation (too many sources) or starvation (too many sinks).

## Step 4 — Tune and simulate
Adjust and model the player progression curve.

## Output
A sources/sinks map, a progression pacing, the imbalance, and a tuned curve.
