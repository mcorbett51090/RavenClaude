## Steps (traverse top-to-bottom; do not skip)
1. Map sources and sinks — Every way currency enters and leaves (§3 #5).
2. Set the pacing — Progression pace against session length and retention.
3. Find the imbalance — Inflation (too many sources) or starvation (too many sinks).
4. Tune and simulate — Adjust and model the player progression curve.
