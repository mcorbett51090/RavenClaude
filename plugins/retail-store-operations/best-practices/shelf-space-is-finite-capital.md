# Shelf space is finite capital

Every facing has a carrying cost; the shelf is finite. Measure space productivity (sales and margin per linear foot or per facing) by SKU and by category. A SKU that doesn't earn its space is borrowing it from one that would. Allocate facings by productivity and role (traffic-driver vs. margin-driver), not by vendor pressure or SKU count. A long tail of duplicative SKUs splits demand and starves the winners — rationalize to the items that own a customer decision.
