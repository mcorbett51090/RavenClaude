# Safety stock buys a service level, and you name the level

Size safety stock to a target in-stock / service level against demand variability and lead-time variability — and state the target. "Add more buffer" with no service-level target is just trapped cash with a story; raising the service level has a named cash cost, so make the trade explicit. The demand-variability model itself is an input you consume, not one you build — set the service-level target and seasonality assumption here and route the statistical model to applied-statistics.
