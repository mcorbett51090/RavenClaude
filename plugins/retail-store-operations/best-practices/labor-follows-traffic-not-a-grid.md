# Labor follows traffic, not a fixed grid

Schedule store labor against the conversion-weighted traffic curve, not a flat headcount grid. Over-staffing the dead hours wastes labor %; under-staffing the peak loses conversion (and conversion loss is invisible on the labor report). Name which one you're trading on every schedule change. Labor % running over plan is usually a scheduling-shape problem before it's a headcount problem — re-shape the hours against traffic before cutting heads, and never cut peak labor to make a weekly labor %.
