# Allocate at the store-SKU level, not the aggregate

"We have enough total" hides a stocked-out store next to an overstocked one. Plan and replenish at the store-SKU level; aggregate availability is a comforting lie during a stockout. When one store sits at 14 weeks of supply while another has been out for two weeks, the fix is a transfer or re-allocation sized by weeks-of-supply, plus a per-store replenishment reset so the imbalance doesn't recur. Always drill from total availability to the store-SKU before declaring inventory "fine".
