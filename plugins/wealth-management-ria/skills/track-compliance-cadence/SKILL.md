# Skill: Track compliance cadence

A missed cadence is exposure regardless of market performance (§3 #6).

## Step 1 — List the cadence items
ADV updates, periodic reviews, required disclosures (§3 #6).

## Step 2 — Schedule against the calendar
Due dates on the regulatory calendar.

## Step 3 — Track due vs overdue
What's coming and what's slipped (§3 #6).

## Step 4 — Route legal questions
Any fiduciary/SEC determination → compliance counsel (§2 #8).

## Output
A compliance-cadence tracker with due/overdue items — ops tracking, legal routed to counsel.
