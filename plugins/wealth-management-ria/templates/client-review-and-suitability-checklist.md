# Client Review & Suitability — Checklist Template

> Output of `advisory-compliance-and-client-review-lead` / the `client-review-and-suitability` skill. A review
> with no suitability re-confirmation and no record is a missed control, not a review.
>
> **Not legal advice.** This is an educational framework and checklist, not a legal conclusion. Firm-specific
> obligations, registration status, and anything legally consequential route to the firm's compliance counsel.

- [ ] **Document the meeting and what was re-confirmed**
