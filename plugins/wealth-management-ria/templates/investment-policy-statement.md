# Investment Policy Statement (IPS) — Template

> Output of `portfolio-analyst` / the `portfolio-construction-and-ips` skill. An IPS with no target ranges,
> no rebalancing rule, or no review trigger is not ready to govern a portfolio.
>
> **Not investment advice.** This is an educational document framework, not a personalized recommendation.
> The licensed adviser completes and applies it after confirming suitability; tax specifics route to a CPA.
