# Rebalancing is a written rule, not a feeling

Pick calendar (rebalance on a schedule) or threshold/bands (rebalance when an asset class drifts past a set range) and write the rule into the IPS. The value of rebalancing is the discipline of selling high and buying low without a discretionary call each time. "Rebalancing" that's really discretionary trading with no documented rule is the anti-pattern. In taxable accounts, prefer bands and rebalance tax-aware (use new contributions and harvested losses first) to keep the trade count and tax drag down.
