## Steps
1. Run the suitability/KYC check: objectives, risk tolerance AND capacity, horizon, liquidity, financial situation, tax status — flag what's missing or stale.
2. Confirm the standard that applies (RIA fiduciary duty vs Reg BI); name conflicts to disclose and manage; route firm-specific determinations to counsel.
3. Build the periodic-review agenda: re-confirm suitability, check the IPS still fits, document the meeting — not just a market update.
4. Check books-and-records: is the basis for advice, the review, the IPS, and the disclosures documented and retained? Apply the marketing-rule lens to any performance/testimonial language.
5. Route next steps: remediate the plan → `financial-planner`; remediate the IPS → `portfolio-analyst`; deep legal questions → `regulatory-compliance` / counsel.
6. Emit the suitability + review output + the Structured Output block, with the `Not investment advice:` and `Client-specific facts to confirm:` lines.
