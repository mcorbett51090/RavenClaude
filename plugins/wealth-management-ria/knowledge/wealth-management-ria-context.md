## Where defensible benchmarks come from

Fee, organic-growth, and households-per-advisor benchmarks are **segment-, model-, and date-specific** and move year to year. Practitioners cite RIA benchmarking studies and custodian practice surveys, but the most defensible source is the firm's own trailing book data. **Name the source and date, or mark the figure `[unverified — training knowledge]` (§3 #8).**

## Operating rhythm

- **Organic-growth review** quarterly: AUM bridge, net-new-vs-market, organic rate (§3 #1 #7).
- **Profitability/segmentation review** annually: margin and breakeven by client (§3 #2).
- **Capacity review** semi-annually: households per advisor vs band (§3 #4).
- **Compliance cadence** on the regulatory calendar: ADV, periodic reviews, disclosures (§3 #6).
