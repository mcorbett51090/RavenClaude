# Wealth Management (RIA Practice) KPI Glossary

> The team's canonical metric definitions. Every metric carries a **definition**, a **window**, and a **baseline** before it ships (CLAUDE.md §3 #1). Benchmark ranges are `[unverified — training knowledge]` unless a dated source is attached — confirm against a current source before using in a deliverable (§3 #8).

## AUM, fees & organic growth

| Metric | Definition | Window | Note |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Net new flows** | New client money − withdrawals | Per period | The growth the practice earned (§3 #1). |
| **Market appreciation** | AUM change from market movement | Per period | Not growth the practice earned (§3 #1 #7). |
| **Organic growth rate** | Net new flows ÷ beginning AUM | Per period | The real health metric (§3 #7). |
| **Blended fee** | Revenue ÷ total AUM | Per period | Off a consistently applied schedule (§3 #3). |
| **Effective fee** | Revenue from a client ÷ that client's AUM | Per client | Inputs client profitability (§3 #2). |

## Clients, capacity & retention

| Metric | Definition | Window | Note |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Client margin** | Client revenue − cost-to-serve | Per client | Rank by this, not AUM (§3 #2). |
| **Breakeven AUM** | Cost-to-serve ÷ effective fee | Per client | Below it, the client loses money (§3 #2). |
| **Households per advisor** | Households ÷ advisors | Point-in-time | Over a band erodes service + retention (§3 #4). |
| **Attrition rate** | Households lost ÷ beginning households | Per period | Compounds; a lost household is lost forever (§3 #5). |

## Compliance cadence

| Item | Definition | Note |
|---|---|---|
| **ADV update** | Form ADV annual/material-change update | On the regulatory calendar (§3 #6). |
| **Periodic review** | Scheduled client review cadence | Tied to client tier + capacity (§3 #4 #6). |
| **Fee-application consistency** | Schedule applied uniformly | Revenue-integrity + disclosure control (§3 #3). |

## The rule

A metric without a **window** and a **baseline** is not a finding — it's a number (§3 #1). A benchmark without a **source URL + retrieval date** is `[unverified — training knowledge]` (§3 #8).
