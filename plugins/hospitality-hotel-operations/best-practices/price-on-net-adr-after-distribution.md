# Price and compare channels on net ADR, not headline rate

A booking is worth its rate minus OTA commission, channel cost, and the loyalty/discount give-back. Compare channels and offers on net-ADR contribution, never gross — a direct booking and a 22%-commission OTA booking at the same headline rate are not the same booking. Drive direct-booking share to cut distribution cost, but treat the OTA as a paid acquisition channel with a known cost, used deliberately, not a leak to plug at any price.
