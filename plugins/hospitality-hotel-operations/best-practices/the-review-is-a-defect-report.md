# The review is a defect report — close the loop

Reputation is the output of operations, not a marketing task. Map every recurring complaint to its operational root cause and a fix (an SOP, a maintenance loop, a staffing change), rank the action list by frequency × score/repeat-rate impact, and route the fix to operations — then re-measure in the next cycle. A review response without an operational change is PR; measuring satisfaction without acting on it is comment-to-archive. The loop only closes when the re-measure confirms the fix.
