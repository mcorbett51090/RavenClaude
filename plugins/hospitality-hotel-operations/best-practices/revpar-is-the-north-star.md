# RevPAR is the north-star, read against GOPPAR

Optimize revenue per available room (RevPAR = ADR × Occupancy), not occupancy or ADR in isolation — a full hotel at a giveaway rate and an empty hotel at rack are both failures. Read RevPAR against GOPPAR (gross operating profit per available room) so you don't buy occupancy with unprofitable cost. An occupancy or ADR win that lowers RevPAR, or a RevPAR win that lowers GOPPAR, is a loss.
