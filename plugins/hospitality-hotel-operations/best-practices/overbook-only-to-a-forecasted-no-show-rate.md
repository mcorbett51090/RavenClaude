# Overbook only to a forecasted no-show rate, with a walk-protocol

Overbooking is a yield tool, but a walk is a broken guarantee. Overbook only to a forecasted, owned no-show/cancellation rate for the date type, and only when the yield upside exceeds the expected walk cost plus brand damage. Never overbook past the walk the property can absorb gracefully, and never without an operations-owned walk-protocol (who walks, where, the comp, the re-accommodation) defined in advance — not improvised at the desk.
