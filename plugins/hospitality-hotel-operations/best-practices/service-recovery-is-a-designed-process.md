# Service recovery is a designed process, not heroics

A defined recovery playbook — acknowledge, own, fix, follow-up — with comp authority tiered to the defect's severity recovers more guests than ad-hoc goodwill, and lets the front line act without escalating every time. The follow-up after the fix is what converts a recovered guest to a loyal one; a comp with no follow-up is just a discount on a bad stay. Recovery left to whoever is working that shift is a coin flip, not a process.
