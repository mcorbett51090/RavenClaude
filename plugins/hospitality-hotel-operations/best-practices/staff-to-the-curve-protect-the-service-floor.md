# Staff to the occupancy curve, protect the service floor

Labor is the biggest controllable cost and the biggest service lever. Schedule front-desk and housekeeping staff to the occupancy forecast — match coverage to the arrival peak and the room-turn load — but never cut below the service floor that a clean room and a staffed desk require. A labor cost win that drops the review score and the repeat rate is a loss that just moved the cost off the P&L and onto the guest.
