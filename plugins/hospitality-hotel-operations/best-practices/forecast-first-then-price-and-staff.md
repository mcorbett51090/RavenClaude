# Forecast first, then price and staff

The demand forecast is the spine of the operation: it drives the rate ladder, the overbooking decision, and the labor schedule. An unforecasted property reacts to booking pace; a forecasted one positions ahead of it. A forecast you produce but never act on is a report, not a tool — it has to change a rate, an overbook, or a schedule. Frame the forecast here; route the statistical method (seasonality, confidence intervals) to `applied-statistics`.
