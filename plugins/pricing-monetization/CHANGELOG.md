# Changelog — pricing-monetization

All notable changes to this plugin are documented here. Versioning is semver, bumped
on every user-visible change and mirrored in `.claude-plugin/marketplace.json`.

## 0.1.0 — 2026-06-26

Initial release. The pricing & monetization team — owns *what to charge and how to
package it*, the question no other RavenClaude plugin answered.

- **2 agents:** `pricing-strategist` (model/metric/packaging/price-change), `monetization-analyst` (WTP research, elasticity, monetization metrics, change-reads).
- **5 skills:** pricing-model-selection, value-metric-design, packaging-and-tiering, willingness-to-pay-research, price-change-rollout.
- **Knowledge bank (3 docs):** pricing decision trees (model / value-metric / WTP-method, Mermaid), monetization-metrics glossary with formulas, dated 2026 reference.
- **10 best-practices** and **3 templates** (packaging worksheet, price-change rollout plan, WTP-study brief).
- Seams: `finance`, `product-management`, `sales-revops`, `applied-statistics`, `fintech-payments-engineering`/`backend-engineering`, `legal-ops-clm`. Requires `ravenclaude-core@>=0.7.0`.
