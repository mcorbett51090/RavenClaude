# Pricing strategy is not legal advice

Price-fixing, resale-price maintenance / MAP enforcement, regional price regulation,
and anti-trust exposure are **legal** questions. This plugin flags them and routes to
counsel (`legal-ops-clm` for contract language); it never issues a legal verdict.
Likewise, pricing recommendations are advisory — they don't guarantee a financial
outcome, and the dollar-impact modeling belongs to `finance`.

**Do:** stop and route to counsel the moment a question touches collusion, MAP, or
price regulation.

**Flag:** any request for a yes/no legal ruling on a pricing-law question — escalate,
don't answer.
