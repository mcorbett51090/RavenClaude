# Freemium is a customer-acquisition cost, not a tier

A free tier is justified only by a *measured conversion path* and a *bounded
cost-to-serve*. "Free because competitors are free" is an unbounded liability, not a
strategy. When either condition is missing, a time-boxed free trial captures the
acquisition benefit without the open-ended cost.

**Do:** require a conversion metric and a cost-to-serve cap before recommending
freemium; otherwise prefer a trial with an end date.

**Flag:** a free tier proposed with no conversion-path evidence or no cost-to-serve bound.
