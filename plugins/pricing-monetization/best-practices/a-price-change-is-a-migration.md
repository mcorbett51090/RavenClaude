# A price change is a migration, not an edit

Existing customers, grandfathering, renewal timing, cohort sequencing, and the comms
plan are part of *every* price change. A new price card with no migration plan is half
a deliverable, and migrating the whole base at once forfeits your chance to read the
churn signal before you're committed.

**Do:** sequence by cohort and renewal date with go/no-go guardrails; grandfather
deliberately; lead with the value narrative before the invoice.

**Flag:** a price increase or repackage shipped with no grandfathering, sequencing, or
guardrail metrics.
