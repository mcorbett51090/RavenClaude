# Realized (net-of-discount) price is the real price

List price is marketing; the street price is the business. When realized ARPA is 35%
below list, your "price" is fiction and discount leakage is a first-class metric, not
an afterthought. Decompose leakage by segment / deal-size / quarter — it almost always
concentrates, and the concentration is the actionable finding.

**Do:** report realized ARPA and decomposed discount leakage alongside any list price.

**Flag:** a pricing claim made on list price while the realized price is unmeasured,
or leakage reported as a single undifferentiated average.
