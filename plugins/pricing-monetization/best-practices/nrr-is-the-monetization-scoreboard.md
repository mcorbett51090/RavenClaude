# NRR is the monetization scoreboard

Net Revenue Retention captures whether the *monetization design* — the value metric,
the expansion paths, the low-end churn — compounds. A model that wins logos but bleeds
NRR is mispriced. Read NRR with GRR (so a few whales don't mask logo churn) and
expansion rate (the signal a good value metric is working).

**Do:** lead a "is our monetization working?" answer with NRR + expansion, then
realized ARPA + leakage, then GRR.

**Flag:** a monetization verdict that leans on growth or new-logo count while ignoring
retention/expansion.
