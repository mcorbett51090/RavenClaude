## 3. PM Task Checklist (per visit type)

### Spring / Cooling Startup

- [ ] Inspect and replace air filters
- [ ] Clean evaporator and condenser coils
- [ ] Check refrigerant charge (EPA 608 required for refrigerant work)
- [ ] Inspect and tighten electrical connections
- [ ] Test contactors, capacitors, relays
- [ ] Lubricate fan motors and bearings
- [ ] Verify thermostat calibration and controls
- [ ] Record equipment operating pressures and temperatures
- [ ] Check condensate drain for obstructions
- [ ] Inspect ductwork connections and insulation (spot check)
- [ ] Document deficiencies and customer sign-off

### Fall / Heating Startup

- [ ] Inspect heat exchanger for cracks (gas furnace/boiler)
- [ ] Clean combustion chamber and burners
- [ ] Check flue venting for blockages and proper draft
- [ ] Test ignition system, safety controls, and limit switches
- [ ] Inspect gas valve, pressure regulator
- [ ] Verify thermostat heating operation
- [ ] Check and replace air filters
- [ ] Record fuel pressure and combustion readings
- [ ] Document deficiencies and customer sign-off

### General / Annual Inspection

- [ ] All spring and fall tasks above
- [ ] Full equipment performance test (temperature differential, static pressure)
- [ ] Inspect refrigerant lines, insulation, and supports
- [ ] Review equipment operating history and failure log with customer
- [ ] Assess equipment age and remaining useful life
- [ ] Recommend capital replacement for units ≥ `[N]` years old or with recurring failures
- [ ] Provide written inspection report to customer
