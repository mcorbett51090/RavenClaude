## Stage Model

| Stage | Definition | Exit Criteria (binary, verifiable) | Default Probability | Forecast Category | Stage Owner |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Prospect / MQL | Initial contact made; basic qualification started | Lead source captured; primary contact identified in CRM; qualification call scheduled | 5% | Omitted | SDR / AE |
| Discovery | Active conversation; pain identified | Pain point documented in Opp notes; budget process confirmed; next step scheduled and future-dated | 15% | Pipeline | AE |
| Technical Validation | Product fit confirmed | Technical win documented; champion confirmed in Contact Role; success criteria agreed | 30% | Best Case | AE + SE |
| Proposal | Written proposal delivered | Proposal sent and confirmed received; decision criteria documented; close date within 90 days | 50% | Best Case | AE |
| Verbal Commit | Buyer agreed to purchase; contract in review | Verbal agreement documented; legal / procurement engaged; contract sent or in review | 75% | Commit | AE |
| Closed Won | Contract signed; order booked | Contract fully executed; order booked in billing system | 100% | Closed Won | AE |
| Closed Lost | Deal lost | Reason documented (min 1 of: Price / Competitor / No decision / Timing / Product gap); loss reason in CRM field | 0% | Closed Lost | AE |

_Replace example exit criteria above with your company's specific, verifiable conditions._
