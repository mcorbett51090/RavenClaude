# creator-economy-operations plugin

> **The business of being a creator** for the RavenClaude marketplace: turning an
> audience into a durable, diversified income. It answers **"how does this audience
> become a business — what's the monetization mix, what are my rates, and how do I
> grow and keep the audience that funds it?"** — the creator's side that
> `marketing-operations` (brand demand-gen), `ecommerce-dtc` (product/fulfillment),
> and `developer-relations` (DevRel) don't own.

**Designed for:** a content creator, newsletter/podcast operator, or creator-manager
who needs the monetization model right, sponsorships priced on value, income
diversified off a single platform, and a sustainable content + audience-growth engine.

## What this plugin gives you

- **A monetization mix that fits the audience** — ads, sponsorships, subscriptions &
  memberships, digital products & courses, affiliate, and services — prioritized by
  audience size × engagement × buying-intent, weighted toward recurring revenue.
- **Sponsorships priced on value, not CPM** — a rate card built from audience value +
  deliverable + separate usage/exclusivity line items + a walk-away floor, with clear
  disclosure.
- **Platform-risk de-risking** — moving audience onto owned channels (email/community)
  and diversifying so no single platform or sponsor is catastrophic to lose.
- **A sustainable content + growth engine** — content pillars, a cadence matched to
  real capacity (not a burnout myth), native repurposing, and an owned-audience funnel,
  steered by engagement and conversion instead of vanity metrics.

## The two agents

| Agent | Owns |
|---|---|
| `creator-business-strategist` | The economics: monetization mix, platform portfolio + platform-risk, audience-funnel/LTV, sponsorship/rate-card valuation, and the creator P&L/MRR. |
| `content-and-audience-manager` | The execution: content strategy & sustainable cadence, platform growth, repurposing, the owned-audience funnel, retention, and analytics. |

## The three skills

| Skill | What's inside |
|---|---|
| `choose-monetization-mix` | Decide which revenue lines to run and in what order, weighted to recurring and away from concentration. |
| `price-a-brand-deal` | Value a sponsorship / build a rate card on value not CPM, with usage/exclusivity priced separately and a walk-away floor. |
| `plan-content-and-audience-growth` | Content pillars + sustainable cadence + native repurposing + the owned-audience funnel, measured by engagement + owned conversion. |

## When to use it

- You have an audience and need to figure out how to actually make money from it.
- A brand made an offer and you need to know if it's fair and what your rates should be.
- Your income depends on one platform or one sponsor and you need to de-risk it.
- You're posting inconsistently or heading for burnout and need a sustainable engine.

## When *not* to use it

- You need brand-side demand-gen / paid media (the advertiser's campaigns) — that's
  `marketing-operations`. This plugin is the creator's side of the deal.
- You're running a real product catalog with inventory and fulfillment — that's
  `ecommerce-dtc`.
- You need DevRel / developer-audience community — that's `developer-relations`.
- You need legal/tax/financial *advice* — see a licensed professional (`finance` for modeling).

## Seams to neighbouring plugins

- **`marketing-operations`** — brand-side demand-gen and paid media.
- **`ecommerce-dtc`** — physical products, inventory, storefront, fulfillment.
- **`developer-relations`** — developer-audience community and docs-as-marketing.
- **`finance`** — creator P&L / tax modeling (verdicts to a licensed professional).
- **`ravenclaude-core`** — the domain-neutral constitution + protocols.
