# Engagement and retention over vanity metrics

**Rule.** Steer by decision metrics — retention/watch-time, engagement rate,
email/product conversion, repeat viewers, churn — not by vanity metrics (impressions,
follower count). Every reported number should attach to a change.

**Why.** Retention and conversion predict a business; raw reach does not. A large
passive audience with no engagement or owned-conversion is worth less than a small
engaged one — and optimizing for impressions can actively hurt the business.

**Smell.** Reporting follower/impression counts with no decision attached; optimizing
for reach while retention and email conversion stall.
