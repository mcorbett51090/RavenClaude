# Price sponsorships on value, not CPM

**Rule.** Price brand deals up from audience value (niche buying-intent, trust,
reach-difficulty), price the deliverable, and charge **separately** for usage /
whitelisting and category exclusivity. Set a walk-away floor. Use rate benchmarks
only as a dated sanity check.

**Why.** A flat CPM ignores that a niche high-intent audience is worth many times a
broad one, and usage/exclusivity rights — often the biggest value — get given away
for free when the deal is priced per-view.

**Smell.** A per-view flat rate with no niche/usage/exclusivity adjustment;
whitelisting rights granted at no extra charge; no walk-away number before negotiating.
