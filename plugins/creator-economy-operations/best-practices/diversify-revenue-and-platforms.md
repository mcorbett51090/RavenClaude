# Diversify revenue and platforms

**Rule.** No single revenue line and no single platform should be catastrophic to
lose. Track revenue concentration (share from one platform, share from one sponsor)
and treat a dangerous concentration as the top priority to fix.

**Why.** One platform or one big sponsor is a single point of failure — an algorithm
change, a policy shift, or a lost contract can zero the income overnight. Resilience
comes from multiple lines and owned channels.

**Smell.** One sponsor is >half of revenue; all income from a single platform's ad
program; no backup if the top line disappears.
