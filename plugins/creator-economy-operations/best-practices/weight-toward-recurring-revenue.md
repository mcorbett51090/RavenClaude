# Weight toward recurring revenue

**Rule.** Wherever the audience supports it, move toward recurring revenue
(memberships/subscriptions) as the stable base, rather than relying on volatile
one-off ad and sponsorship income. Name an MRR target.

**Why.** Ad RPM and sponsorship budgets swing with seasonality, algorithms, and the
ad market; recurring revenue smooths the notorious income volatility of a creator
business and is what makes it a business, not a series of paydays.

**Smell.** 100% of income from ads + sponsorships with no recurring line; income that
swings wildly month to month with no floor.
