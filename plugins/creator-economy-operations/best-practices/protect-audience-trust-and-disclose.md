# Protect audience trust and disclose

**Rule.** Order monetization so trust-spending lines (heavy sponsorship, aggressive
affiliate) never outpace trust-building, and disclose all sponsored/affiliate content
clearly, conspicuously, and in-content. A brand that wants disclosure hidden is a red flag.

**Why.** Audience trust is the only asset that compounds; over-monetization or shilling
low-quality products draws it down permanently. Clear disclosure is both legally
required (FTC/ASA and equivalents — dated, jurisdictional) and a trust protection.

**Smell.** Back-to-back sponsorships for products the creator wouldn't use; disclosure
buried in a description or omitted; promoting anything that pays regardless of fit.
