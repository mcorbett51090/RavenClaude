# Date platform and rate facts

**Rule.** Platform payout/RPM terms, monetization eligibility, sponsorship rate
benchmarks, algorithm behavior, and disclosure rules are volatile and jurisdictional.
Cite each with a retrieval date, or mark `[unverified — training knowledge]` and
verify before a commitment.

**Why.** A payout rate or rate benchmark recalled from training is stale by the time
it's quoted, and a misread disclosure rule creates legal exposure. Durable structure
(revenue models, the rate-card method, platform-risk framing) doesn't need dates; the
numbers and rules do.

**Smell.** "This platform pays $X RPM" or "sponsorships go for $Y" with no date; a
disclosure claim stated without checking the creator's jurisdiction.

**Cite:** plugin §4.7; the marketplace accuracy discipline (`AGENTS.md`); the dated map
in `knowledge/creator-platforms-and-monetization-2026.md`.
