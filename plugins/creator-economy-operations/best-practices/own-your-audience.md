# Own your audience

**Rule.** Weight every plan toward moving audience onto **owned** channels (email
list, community, website) and away from dependence on **rented** algorithmic reach.
Every rented-platform post should earn a subscribe.

**Why.** A rented platform can change payout, throttle reach, or suspend an account
with no recourse. An email list/community is not intermediated — it's the most
valuable, most durable asset a creator has.

**Smell.** 100% of audience on one platform with no email list; growth measured only
in followers with no owned-audience capture.
