# Choose native vs cross-platform by the app, not preference

Pick native (Swift + Kotlin) when the app needs heavy platform-specific UX, top-tier performance, or immediate access to new OS features; pick React Native or Flutter when it's a largely-shared business app built by one team that values shared iteration. Name the trade you're accepting either way — cross-platform buys shared code and pays at the native boundary and the last 5% of platform-native UX. Choosing by team habit instead of app need is a cost you didn't price.
