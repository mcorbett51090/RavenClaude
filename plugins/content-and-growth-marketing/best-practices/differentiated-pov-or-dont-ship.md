# A differentiated POV or don't ship

Me-too content that restates the first page of the SERP, generic email blasts, and copy with no angle add nothing and rank/convert for nothing. Every asset needs a defensible reason to exist — original data, hard-won experience, a contrarian take, a genuinely better explanation. If the only differentiator is "we also wrote about it," don't publish: find the angle, repurpose existing depth, or skip the slot. A skipped low-value piece costs less than a published one that dilutes the brand.
