# Deliverability is the foundation

An email in the spam folder converts at zero, no matter how good the copy. Authentication (SPF, DKIM, DMARC), list hygiene with an active sunset policy for the unengaged, sender-reputation management, and healthy engagement signals come before clever subject lines. Sending to people who don't open trains mailbox providers to bury the whole list — so a smaller engaged list out-delivers and out-converts a big stale one. Fix authentication and prune the unengaged before optimizing the message.
