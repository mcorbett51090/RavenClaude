# Segment and trigger, don't blast

A behavior-triggered, segmented message beats a batch-and-blast to the whole list on every metric — and protects deliverability by raising engagement. Design the journey: the trigger, the entry/exit criteria, the branching on behavior, and the suppression rules so people aren't stuck in or double-messaged across flows. If you can't name the trigger and the exit, it's a broadcast, not a flow. Reserve true broadcasts (newsletter, announcement) for genuinely list-wide messages, and even then segment by engagement and suppress the unengaged.
