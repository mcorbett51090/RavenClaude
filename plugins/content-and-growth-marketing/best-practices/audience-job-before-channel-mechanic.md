# Audience job before channel mechanic

Start from the reader/searcher/subscriber's job-to-be-done and their funnel stage, then pick the keyword, the format, and the send. Content, SEO, and email that target a channel mechanic (a high-volume keyword, a trending format, a calendar slot) but not a real audience job rank-and-bounce or send-and-burn. Every asset names the job it serves; if you can't name the job, you're optimizing the mechanic, not the outcome.
