# Strategy compounds; tactics decay

The deliverable is an asset base that grows in value over time — topic clusters and pillar pages, an owned internal-link topology, a lifecycle system of triggered flows — not a stream of one-off posts, audits, and email blasts that reset every week. Before shipping anything, ask whether it builds a durable, compounding asset or is a one-off that decays the week it ships. Prefer the structure that compounds; kill the filler that doesn't.
