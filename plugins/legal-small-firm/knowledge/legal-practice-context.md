# Small-firm practice benchmarks & context (2025)

Dated practice-economics benchmarks for a small-firm engagement, plus the professional-responsibility frame. Operational guidance, not legal advice — legal authority routes to the attorney.

## The realization cascade (Clio 2025)

Clio's 2025 Legal Trends pegs the average law-firm utilization at ~38% (~3.0 billable hours captured in an 8-hour day), realization at ~88%, and collection at ~93% — so the typical lawyer banks ~2.4 of 8 hours. The cascade (utilization → realization → collection) is where the money leaks; realization and the billed-vs-collected gap are the truth (§3 #1, #7).

## Lockup & revenue (Clio 2025)

Median lockup runs ~43 days realization, ~32 days collection, ~93 days total — A/R is part of the matter, not after it. Billable revenue benchmarks: ~$83,219 for solos and ~$156,963 for small firms (2–4 staff). Un-scoped hourly matters are the most common realization leak (§3 #4).

## Ethics & not legal advice

Trust accounting, confidentiality, conflicts, and the rules of professional conduct are non-negotiable hard guardrails, routed to the attorney and the applicable bar rules (§3 #6). Everything this plugin produces is attorney decision-support and work product — it does not advise clients, appear, or form an attorney-client relationship (§3 #3).
