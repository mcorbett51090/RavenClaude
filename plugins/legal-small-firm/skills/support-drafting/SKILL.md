# Skill: Support drafting as work product

Drafts are the attorney's tool, not advice to a client.

## Step 1 — Scope the document
Purpose, parties, and the deal terms (§3 #4).

## Step 2 — Draft from the library
Assemble a first draft from vetted clauses (§3 #3).

## Step 3 — Flag the issues
Surface risks, missing terms, and decisions for the attorney.

## Step 4 — Hand to the attorney
Present as work product for review, revision, and adoption.

## Output
A first-draft or redline with issue flags, presented as attorney work product.
