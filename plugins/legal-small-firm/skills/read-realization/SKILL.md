# Skill: Read realization

Billed hours that aren't collected are fiction.

## Step 1 — Compute realization
Collected ÷ standard value; and billed-vs-collected (§3 #1).

## Step 2 — Locate the leakage
Write-downs (pre-bill), write-offs (post-bill), and A/R aging.

## Step 3 — Attribute the cause
Scope creep, un-budgeted matters, or collection follow-through (§3 #4, #7).

## Step 4 — Act
Map the fix to the leakage source.

## Output
A realization read, the billed-vs-collected gap, the leakage source, and a fix.
