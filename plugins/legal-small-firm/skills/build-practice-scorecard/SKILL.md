# Skill: Build a practice scorecard

The scorecard makes the practice's economics visible.

## Step 1 — Lead with realization
Defined as collected ÷ standard value, with baseline (§3 #1).

## Step 2 — Add utilization
Billable-hour ratio against the non-billable load (§3 #5).

## Step 3 — Add collected revenue and A/R
Collected revenue and A/R aging (§3 #7).

## Step 4 — Add intake health
Decline rate and matter-fit at intake (§3 #2).

## Output
A realization-led practice scorecard with utilization, collections, A/R, and intake health, each baselined.
