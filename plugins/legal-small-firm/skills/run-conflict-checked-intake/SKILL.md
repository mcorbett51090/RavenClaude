# Skill: Run conflict-checked intake

The worst client is the one you should have declined.

## Step 1 — Conflict check
Screen for conflicts against existing/former clients (§3 #2).

## Step 2 — Fit and viability
Assess the matter's merit, the client, and the economics.

## Step 3 — Scope and fee
Set the scope and the fee structure deliberately (§3 #4).

## Step 4 — Decide
Recommend engage/decline, routing the call to the attorney.

## Output
A conflict screen, a fit/viability read, a scoped fee structure, and an engage/decline recommendation.
