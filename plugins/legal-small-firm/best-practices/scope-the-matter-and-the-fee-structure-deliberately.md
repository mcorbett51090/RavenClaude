# Scope the matter and the fee structure deliberately

**Status:** Pattern. **Constitution:** §3, §4.

## Use when
Any small-firm legal practice deliverable where this question is in play.

## The rule
Hourly, flat, and contingency each shift risk differently; an un-scoped matter on an open-ended hourly with no budget is where write-offs are born.

## Why
This is house opinion §3 #4. Getting it wrong is the most common way a small-firm legal practice analysis misleads the operator who acts on it.

## How to apply
- Apply it before reaching for a method.
- Make the relevant number carry a definition, window, and baseline (§3 #1).
- Cite the source + date for any external figure, or mark it `[unverified — training knowledge]`.
