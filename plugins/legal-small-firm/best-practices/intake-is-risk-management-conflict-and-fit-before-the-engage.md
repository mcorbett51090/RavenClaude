# Intake is risk management — conflict and fit before the engagement

**Status:** Absolute rule. **Constitution:** §3, §4.

## Use when
Any small-firm legal practice deliverable where this question is in play.

## The rule
A conflict check and a fit/viability screen at intake prevent the matters that destroy realization and create malpractice and ethics risk; the worst client is the one you should have declined.

## Why
This is house opinion §3 #2. Getting it wrong is the most common way a small-firm legal practice analysis misleads the operator who acts on it.
