# Trade-contracting KPI glossary

The metrics a trade contractor is judged on — formulas, the misreads, and where a figure needs a date or a `[unverified]` mark.

## Job & pricing metrics

Loaded labor rate, flat-rate price, job gross margin, material markup, average ticket. The loaded rate is the master estimating number (§3 #1, #2).

## Field metrics

Billable-hour ratio, first-time-fix/callback rate, revenue-per-truck-per-day, jobs-per-day (§3 #3, #4, #6).

## Sales metrics

Quote close rate, average ticket, option-presentation take-rate, lead-to-sale (§3 #7).

## Sourcing note

Figures in this file are from the author's domain knowledge and are marked `[unverified — training knowledge]` or `[ESTIMATE]` at point of use. Validate against a primary source before putting any figure in a client deliverable (§3 cite-or-mark rule).
