# Price service on a flat-rate book, not guessed hours

**Status:** Absolute rule. **Constitution:** §3, §4.

## Use when
Any skilled trades contracting deliverable where this question is in play.

## The rule
Flat-rate pricing protects margin from the slow tech and the unexpected, and it's what the customer expects; time-and-materials on service work leaks margin and breeds disputes.

## Why
This is house opinion §3 #2. Getting it wrong is the most common way a skilled trades contracting analysis misleads the operator who acts on it.

## How to apply
- Apply it before reaching for a method.
- Make the relevant number carry a definition, window, and baseline (§3 #1).
- Cite the source + date for any external figure, or mark it `[unverified — training knowledge]`.
