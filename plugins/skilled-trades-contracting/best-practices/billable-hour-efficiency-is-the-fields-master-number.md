# Billable-hour efficiency is the field's master number

**Status:** Pattern. **Constitution:** §3, §4.

## Use when
Any skilled trades contracting deliverable where this question is in play.

## The rule
Revenue is technicians × billable hours × rate; non-billable time (drive, restock, rework) is the silent margin killer. Measure billable-hour ratio before headcount.

## Why
This is house opinion §3 #3. Getting it wrong is the most common way a skilled trades contracting analysis misleads the operator who acts on it.

## How to apply
- Apply it before reaching for a method.
- Make the relevant number carry a definition, window, and baseline (§3 #1).
- Cite the source + date for any external figure, or mark it `[unverified — training knowledge]`.
