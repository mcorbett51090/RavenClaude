# First-time-fix and callback rate are margin, not just quality

**Status:** Pattern. **Constitution:** §3, §4.

## Use when
Any skilled trades contracting deliverable where this question is in play.

## The rule
A callback is a free truck roll — it doubles the labor cost of a job and burns the schedule; first-time-fix rate is a financial metric.

## Why
This is house opinion §3 #4. Getting it wrong is the most common way a skilled trades contracting analysis misleads the operator who acts on it.

## How to apply
- Apply it before reaching for a method.
- Make the relevant number carry a definition, window, and baseline (§3 #1).
- Cite the source + date for any external figure, or mark it `[unverified — training knowledge]`.
