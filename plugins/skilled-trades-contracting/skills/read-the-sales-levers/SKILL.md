# Skill: Read the sales levers

More leads rarely fix a margin problem.

## Step 1 — Measure close rate and ticket
Quotes won ÷ presented; average invoice (§3 #7).

## Step 2 — Find the revenue gap
The revenue available from lifting close rate or ticket.

## Step 3 — Improve option-presentation
Good/better/best and financing at the point of quote.

## Step 4 — Compare to lead spend
Weigh the levers against more marketing.

## Output
A close-rate and average-ticket read, the revenue gap, and the presentation levers.
