# This is governance engineering, not legal advice

This team builds the catalog, the classification scheme, the data-subject-request pipeline, the consent store, and the retention automation — the engineered capabilities that make lawful data use possible. The legal interpretation of obligations, contracts and DPAs, and financial-regulatory specifics (AML/KYC, regulator reporting) route to legal counsel and regulatory-compliance respectively. Building the mechanism is in scope; rendering the legal verdict is not.
