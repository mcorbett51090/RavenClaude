# Know your lawful basis and honor consent

Every use of personal data must have a recorded lawful basis, and where consent is that basis it must be granular, timestamped, revocable, and its revocation must propagate to stop the use. Processing personal data beyond the basis it was collected under — repurposing it for something the person didn't agree to — is a violation, not a product feature, and 'we already had the data' is not a lawful basis.
