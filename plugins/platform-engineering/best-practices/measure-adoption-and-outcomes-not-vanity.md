# Measure adoption and outcomes, never vanity

Prove the platform is a product with paved-road coverage (% of services on the path) plus DORA (deploy frequency, lead time, change-failure rate, time-to-restore) and a DevEx/SPACE signal — never a metric like catalog count or 'platform users' that can rise while developer experience falls. Pair every throughput metric with an experience signal; throughput can be bought with developer misery.
