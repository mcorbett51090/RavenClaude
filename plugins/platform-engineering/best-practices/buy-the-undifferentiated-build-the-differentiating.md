# Buy the undifferentiated, build the differentiating

Portal, catalog, and scaffolder are mostly undifferentiated heavy lifting — buy or adopt-OSS them; build only the thin glue that encodes *your* organisation's opinions (the golden-path templates, the guardrail defaults, the catalog model). Before recommending "we'll just self-host Backstage", name the real total cost of ownership: a framework you self-host is a product you now staff a team to own. Spend your scarce platform engineers on the differentiating opinions, not on re-implementing a catalog UI.
