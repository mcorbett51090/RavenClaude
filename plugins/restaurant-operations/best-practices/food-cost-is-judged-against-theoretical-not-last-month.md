# Food cost is judged against theoretical, not last month

**Status:** Absolute rule. **Constitution:** §3, §4.

## Use when
Any restaurant operations deliverable where this question is in play.

## The rule
Actual food cost (industry average ~32.4% for full-service, target 28–35%) only means something next to the recipe-derived theoretical; the gap is waste, theft, portioning, or price.

## Why
This is house opinion §3 #2. Getting it wrong is the most common way a restaurant operations analysis misleads the operator who acts on it.

## How to apply
- Apply it before reaching for a method.
- Make the relevant number carry a definition, window, and baseline (§3 #1).
- Cite the source + date for any external figure, or mark it `[unverified — training knowledge]`.
