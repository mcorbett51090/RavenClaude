# Prime cost is the master number

**Status:** Absolute rule. **Constitution:** §3, §4.

## Use when
Any restaurant operations deliverable where this question is in play.

## The rule
Food cost plus labor cost is the one figure that runs the business; benchmarks land at 55–65% of revenue (QSR ~55–60%, full-service 60–65%). Read either half alone and you'll fix the wrong cost.

## Why
This is house opinion §3 #1. Getting it wrong is the most common way a restaurant operations analysis misleads the operator who acts on it.

## How to apply
- Apply it before reaching for a method.
- Make the relevant number carry a definition, window, and baseline (§3 #1).
- Cite the source + date for any external figure, or mark it `[unverified — training knowledge]`.
