# Labor is a ratio to sales, with a floor

**Status:** Pattern. **Constitution:** §3, §4.

## Use when
Any restaurant operations deliverable where this question is in play.

## The rule
Labor % only reads against the daypart's sales; cutting below the service line trades a labor point for a guest-experience and turnover cost that's larger.

## Why
This is house opinion §3 #4. Getting it wrong is the most common way a restaurant operations analysis misleads the operator who acts on it.

## How to apply
- Apply it before reaching for a method.
- Make the relevant number carry a definition, window, and baseline (§3 #1).
- Cite the source + date for any external figure, or mark it `[unverified — training knowledge]`.
