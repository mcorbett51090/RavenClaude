# Restaurant market & benchmarks (2025–2026)

Dated cost benchmarks for a four-wall read. Food and labor moved sharply post-2020 — every figure carries a source and a retrieval date.

## Food cost

Industry benchmark 28–35%; full-service average ~32.4% (QSR 28–32%, casual 30–34%, fine dining 32–35%). Food costs are now >35% above pre-pandemic levels — a stale benchmark misleads (§3 #8).

## Prime cost

Typically 55–65% of revenue: QSR ~55–60%, fast-casual ~58–63%, full-service ~60–65%, fine dining often above 65%.

## Industry scale

The U.S. restaurant industry generates ~$1T in annual revenue across ~618,000 establishments — useful for sizing, not for any single unit's targets.
