# Restaurant unit economics

How a restaurant makes money — prime cost, the menu matrix, labor as a ratio, and the controls.

## Prime cost & the margin bridge

Food + labor at 55–65% of revenue is the master number; the rest of the P&L is rent, utilities, and royalties (§3 #1).

## Menu engineering

Contribution margin in dollars × popularity drives the mix; price cuts rarely fix margin (§3 #3, #5).

## Controls

Comps, voids, and waste are a control system; their rates and authorizers are first-class (§3 #6).
