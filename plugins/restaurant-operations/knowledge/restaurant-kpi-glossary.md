# Restaurant KPI glossary

The metrics a four-wall P&L is judged on — formulas, the misreads, and where a benchmark needs a date.

## Cost metrics

Prime cost (food + labor ÷ revenue), food cost %, labor cost %, theoretical vs actual food cost. Prime cost is the master number (§3 #1).

## Sales & throughput metrics

Covers, average check, sales per labor hour, table turns, speed of service, comps/voids/waste rates.

## Profit metrics

Contribution margin per item, four-wall EBITDA, and the format-matched benchmarks they read against.
