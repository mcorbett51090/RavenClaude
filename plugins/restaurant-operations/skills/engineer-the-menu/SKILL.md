# Skill: Engineer the menu

Menu engineering raises margin by moving the mix toward high-contribution items, not by discounting.

## Step 1 — Cost each item to contribution margin
Recipe cost to theoretical, then price minus cost = contribution margin in dollars (§3 #5).

## Step 2 — Map the matrix
Plot margin × popularity: stars, plow-horses, puzzles, dogs.

## Step 3 — Move the mix
Reposition/redesign to push stars and fix or cut dogs; menu layout and naming move mix more than price.

## Step 4 — Re-measure
Track contribution margin per cover before and after.

## Output
A four-quadrant menu map and a mix-shift plan with the margin-per-cover impact.
