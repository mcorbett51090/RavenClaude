# Skill: Lift recommended-care compliance

Low acceptance is usually communication, not demand.

## Step 1 — Measure accepted vs recommended
Track acceptance for each recommended-care category (§3 #4).

## Step 2 — Find the drop-off
Identify where the recommendation fails — exam-room language, estimate, or follow-up.

## Step 3 — Standardize presentation
Tie acceptance to a consistent protocol and estimate (§3 #1).

## Step 4 — Re-measure
Track acceptance before and after; frame as both outcome and revenue.

## Output
An acceptance read by category, the drop-off point, a presentation fix, and the before/after.
