# Skill: Instrument production and ACT

Revenue is two engines; this reads both before prescribing.

## Step 1 — Compute production per DVM
Total production ÷ doctor-FTEs, by doctor (§3 #2).

## Step 2 — Compute ACT and visits
Average client transaction × visit volume — the other half of revenue.

## Step 3 — Decompose a revenue move
Attribute a change to doctors, production-per-doctor, ACT, or visits — each has a different fix.

## Step 4 — Baseline it
Every figure carries a window and a baseline (§3 #1).

## Output
A two-engine revenue decomposition with the driver of any move named and baselined.
