# Skill: Unlock schedule capacity

Capacity gates revenue; this finds the constraint and relieves it.

## Step 1 — Map utilization
Measure schedule utilization and the doctor bottleneck (§3 #3).

## Step 2 — Redesign the template
Adjust appointment lengths/types and the support hand-off to add doctor throughput.

## Step 3 — Set the support ratio
Tune the tech-to-doctor ratio so doctors work at the top of their license (§3 #7).

## Step 4 — Quantify the unlock
State the added capacity and revenue from the change.

## Output
A capacity read, an appointment-template redesign, a support-ratio recommendation, and the revenue unlock.
