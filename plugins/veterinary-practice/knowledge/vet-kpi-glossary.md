# Veterinary practice KPI glossary

The metrics a veterinary practice is judged on — formulas, the misreads, and where a benchmark needs a date.

## Revenue metrics

Production per DVM, average client transaction (ACT), visits, new-client rate, revenue per FTE. Revenue is production × ACT × visits (§3 #2).

## Operational metrics

Schedule utilization, doctor-to-support ratio, recommended-care compliance/acceptance, no-show rate.

## Profit metrics

Gross/net margin, staff cost % of revenue, and the consolidation-market context they read against (§3 #5).
