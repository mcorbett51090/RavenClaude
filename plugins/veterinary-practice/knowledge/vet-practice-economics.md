# Veterinary practice economics

How a practice makes money — the revenue engines, capacity, compliance, and the cost stack.

## The revenue engines

Doctors × production-per-doctor, and ACT × visits — diagnose both (§3 #2).

## Capacity & staffing

The schedule and the support ratio gate revenue; retention is an operations metric (§3 #3, #7).

## Compliance & pricing

Recommended-care acceptance is revenue and outcome; fees price from the cost stack (§3 #4, #6).
