# Veterinary market & consolidation (2025–2026)

Dated market context for a position decision. Consolidation is fast — every figure carries a source and a retrieval date.

## Market size & growth

The veterinary services market was ~$145.65B in 2024, projected toward ~$281.93B by 2033 (~7.66% CAGR). North America held ~37–42% of revenue.

## Consolidation

Corporate chains held ~40.92% of 2025 revenue; PE ownership rose from ~8% of U.S. clinics in 2011 toward ~50% by 2025. Mars alone operates ~3,000 clinics after the ~$9.1B VCA deal (§3 #5).

## Independent position

Independents still hold ~51% of sites, competing on personalized care and community ties — position to this, not nostalgia (§3 #5).
