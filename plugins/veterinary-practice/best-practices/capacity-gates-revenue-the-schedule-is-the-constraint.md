# Capacity gates revenue — the schedule is the constraint

**Status:** Pattern. **Constitution:** §3, §4.

## Use when
Any veterinary practice deliverable where this question is in play.

## The rule
A fully-booked schedule with a doctor bottleneck caps revenue regardless of demand; the doctor-to-support-staff ratio and appointment template are first-class operational levers.

## Why
This is house opinion §3 #3. Getting it wrong is the most common way a veterinary practice analysis misleads the operator who acts on it.

## How to apply
- Apply it before reaching for a method.
- Make the relevant number carry a definition, window, and baseline (§3 #1).
