# Production per DVM and ACT are the revenue engine

**Status:** Absolute rule. **Constitution:** §3, §4.

## Use when
Any veterinary practice deliverable where this question is in play.

## The rule
Practice revenue is doctors × production per doctor, and average client transaction × visits. Read either without the other and you misdiagnose a revenue problem.

## Why
This is house opinion §3 #2. Getting it wrong is the most common way a veterinary practice analysis misleads the operator who acts on it.

## How to apply
- Apply it before reaching for a method.
- Make the relevant number carry a definition, window, and baseline (§3 #1).
- Cite the source + date for any external figure, or mark it `[unverified — training knowledge]`.
