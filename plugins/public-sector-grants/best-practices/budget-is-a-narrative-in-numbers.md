# The budget is a narrative in numbers

Every budget line is justified in the budget narrative, tied to a logic-model activity, and passes the allowable/allocable/reasonable test. The budget table and the narrative must agree to the dollar — a mismatch is an instant credibility hit and an audit risk. A number with no narrative justification is a finding waiting to happen. Decide allowability, the indirect-cost rate, and match/cost-share as the budget is built, not after the award.
