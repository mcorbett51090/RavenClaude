# Answer the funder's question, in the funder's order, in the funder's words

The NOFO/RFP review and scoring criteria are the rubric the application is judged against. Mirror its structure, order, and language, and address each weighted criterion explicitly. A brilliant narrative that doesn't map to a criterion scores zero on that criterion — points come from addressing what the funder weights, not from prose quality. Research what the funder actually funds and rewards, not what the org wishes they funded.
