# Allowable, allocable, reasonable — all three, every cost

2 CFR Part 200 is the test for every federal dollar. **Allowable**: permitted by the cost principles and the award terms, not a prohibited/excluded item. **Allocable**: benefits the award in proportion to the amount charged. **Reasonable**: a prudent person would incur it, consistent with market and policy. A cost that fails any one of the three is unallowable, full stop — "mission-critical" is not an allowability category. Costs must also fall within the period of performance and be contemporaneously documented.
