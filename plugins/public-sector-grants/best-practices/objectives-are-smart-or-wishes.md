# Objectives are SMART or they're wishes

Specific, Measurable, Achievable, Relevant, Time-bound — each objective carries a baseline, a target, a measure, and a deadline. "Improve outcomes" is not an objective; "increase third-grade reading proficiency by 15% by 2028, measured by the state assessment" is. A reviewer can't score an aspiration, and an evaluator can't prove one. Every objective also implies the data the evaluation plan must collect to demonstrate it.
