# Fund the mission, don't chase the money

Assess fit before fundability. A grant you win that pulls the org off-mission, can't be staffed, or dies the day the period of performance ends is a loss, not a win — it commits the org to deliver something off-mission under audit. Mission alignment, eligibility, organizational capacity, and a plausible sustainability tail are go/no-go inputs, not afterthoughts. The dollar amount on offer is the least important variable.
