# Co-sell dies without a named rep-to-rep motion (§3 #3)

**Rule.** A co-sell motion needs mapped account overlap, individual reps named on both
sides, and a shared incentive each rep feels. "We'll co-sell" with none of these is a
press release.

**Why.** Co-sell happens between two people who each have a reason to pick up the phone.
No overlap means nothing to co-sell; no shared incentive means no one prioritizes it.

**Do**
- Gate the motion on real account overlap — no overlap, no co-sell.
- Pair specific reps for target accounts and give them a shared incentive (SPIFF, quota relief, referral fee).
- Prove the motion on three named accounts before scaling.

**Don't**
- Announce a co-sell partnership with no account map and no named reps.
- Rely on goodwill instead of an incentive.
