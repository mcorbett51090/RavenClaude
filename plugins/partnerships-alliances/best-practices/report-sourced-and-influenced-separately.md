# Report partner-sourced and partner-influenced separately (§3 #1)

**Rule.** Sourced (the partner originated the opportunity) and influenced (the partner
touched a deal direct already had) are two different lines. Write the attribution rule
down before you report a number, and never sum them into one "partner pipeline" figure.

**Why.** Conflating them inflates program ROI and can double-count revenue already
credited to a direct rep's quota. The first time finance catches an influenced deal
reported as sourced, the whole program number loses credibility.

**Do**
- Define sourced vs influenced in one written rule; ambiguous deals default to influenced.
- Report sourced ARR against a baseline, and influenced separately.
- Reconcile sourced deals against direct quota attainment (seam: `sales-revops`).

**Don't**
- Claim a "partner pipeline" number that quietly blends both.
- Change the attribution rule mid-period to flatter the result.
