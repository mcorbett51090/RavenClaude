## Rating scale (impact × likelihood)
| Rating | Meaning | Action expectation |
|---|---|---|
| **High** | Material exposure; systemic or high-likelihood control failure | Prompt remediation; reported to the audit committee |
| **Medium** | Meaningful exposure; action plan with owner + date | Remediate within agreed timeframe |
| **Low** | Minor / isolated | Track; may batch or accept with rationale |
