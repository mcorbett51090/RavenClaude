# LCOE and project IRR are different questions — show both

**Status:** Absolute rule. **Constitution:** §3, §4.

## Use when
Any renewable energy deliverable where this question is in play.

## The rule
Levelized cost of energy prices the energy; project IRR prices the equity over the hold. Cost-per-watt (~$2.56 in 2025) feeds both but answers neither alone.

## Why
This is house opinion §3 #1. Getting it wrong is the most common way a renewable energy analysis misleads the operator who acts on it.

## How to apply
- Apply it before reaching for a method.
- Make the relevant number carry a definition, window, and baseline (§3 #1).
- Cite the source + date for any external figure, or mark it `[unverified — training knowledge]`.
