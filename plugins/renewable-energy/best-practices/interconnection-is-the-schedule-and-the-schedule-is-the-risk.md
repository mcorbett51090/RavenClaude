# Interconnection is the schedule, and the schedule is the risk

**Status:** Absolute rule. **Constitution:** §3, §4.

## Use when
Any renewable energy deliverable where this question is in play.

## The rule
The interconnection queue, study costs, and upgrade allocations gate most projects more than construction does — model the queue, not just the build.

## Why
This is house opinion §3 #2. Getting it wrong is the most common way a renewable energy analysis misleads the operator who acts on it.

## How to apply
- Apply it before reaching for a method.
- Make the relevant number carry a definition, window, and baseline (§3 #1).
- Cite the source + date for any external figure, or mark it `[unverified — training knowledge]`.
