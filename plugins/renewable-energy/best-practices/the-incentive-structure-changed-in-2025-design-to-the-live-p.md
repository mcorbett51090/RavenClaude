# The incentive structure changed in 2025 — design to the live pathway

**Status:** Pattern. **Constitution:** §3, §4.

## Use when
Any renewable energy deliverable where this question is in play.

## The rule
Residential 25D ended Dec 31, 2025; third-party ownership (leases, PPAs, prepaid) remains eligible under 48E through 2027. Structure to the pathway that's actually available, with a date.

## Why
This is house opinion §3 #3. Getting it wrong is the most common way a renewable energy analysis misleads the operator who acts on it.

## How to apply
- Apply it before reaching for a method.
- Make the relevant number carry a definition, window, and baseline (§3 #1).
- Cite the source + date for any external figure, or mark it `[unverified — training knowledge]`.
