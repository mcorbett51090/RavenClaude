# Renewables KPI glossary

The metrics a solar/storage project is judged on — formulas, the misreads, and where a benchmark needs a date.

## Economic metrics

LCOE, project IRR (levered/unlevered), net cost after incentives, cost-per-watt (~$2.56 in 2025), payback. LCOE and IRR answer different questions (§3 #1).

## Schedule & resource metrics

Interconnection-queue position, P50/P90 energy yield, capacity factor, development-milestone schedule (§3 #2, #6).

## Operating metrics

Availability, degradation rate, O&M cost, inverter-replacement cycle, storage dispatch value (§3 #5, #7).
