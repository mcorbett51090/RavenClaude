# Skill: Read asset performance over life

A solar asset is a 25-year machine; the life drives the IRR.

## Step 1 — Measure availability and yield
Actual vs P50/P90 expected production (§3 #6).

## Step 2 — Track degradation
Module degradation and inverter health over time (§3 #5).

## Step 3 — Read O&M cost
O&M and replacement cost against the pro-forma.

## Step 4 — Reforecast the IRR
Update the return with realized performance.

## Output
An availability/yield read, a degradation track, an O&M-vs-pro-forma view, and a reforecast IRR.
