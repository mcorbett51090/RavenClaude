# Skill: Model LCOE and project IRR

Cost-per-watt feeds both but answers neither alone.

## Step 1 — Build LCOE
Lifetime cost ÷ lifetime energy, on net cost after incentives (§3 #1, #4).

## Step 2 — Build project IRR
Levered and unlevered equity return over the hold (§3 #1).

## Step 3 — Size on P90
Use the P90 production estimate for financing, not P50 (§3 #6).

## Step 4 — Include the life
Add degradation, inverter replacement, and O&M over 25 years (§3 #5).

## Output
An LCOE, a levered/unlevered IRR on net cost, sized to P90, with the 25-year cost profile.
