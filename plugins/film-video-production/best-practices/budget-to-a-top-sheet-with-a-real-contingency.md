# Budget to a top-sheet with a real contingency

**Status:** Absolute rule. **Constitution:** §3, §4.

## Use when
Any film & video production deliverable where this question is in play.

## The rule
The top-sheet (above/below-the-line, post, contingency) is the budget's spine; a number without contingency and a defensible build is a wish, not a budget. [unverified — training knowledge]

## Why
This is house opinion §3 #1. Getting it wrong is the most common way a film & video production analysis misleads the operator who acts on it.

## How to apply
- Apply it before reaching for a method.
- Make the relevant number carry a definition, window, and baseline (§3 #1).
- Cite the source + date for any external figure, or mark it `[unverified — training knowledge]`.
