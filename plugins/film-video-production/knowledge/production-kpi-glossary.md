# Production KPI glossary

The metrics a production is judged on — formulas, the misreads, and where a figure needs a `[unverified]` mark.

## Budget metrics

Top-sheet categories, cost-vs-bid variance, contingency burn, below-the-line build. The top-sheet is the spine (§3 #1, #4).

## Schedule metrics

Shoot days, pages-per-day, company moves, turnaround compliance, cover days (§3 #2).

## Post & delivery metrics

Post critical-path days, picture-lock date, deliverable completeness, QC pass rate (§3 #3, #6).

## Sourcing note

Figures in this file are from the author's domain knowledge and are marked `[unverified — training knowledge]` or `[ESTIMATE]` at point of use. Validate against a primary source before putting any figure in a client deliverable (§3 cite-or-mark rule).
