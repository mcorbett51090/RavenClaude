# Skill: Schedule the shoot

A calendar that ignores location grouping burns money on the day.

## Step 1 — Break down the days
Scenes into shoot days by page count and complexity (§3 #2).

## Step 2 — Group by location and cast
Minimize company moves and respect availability.

## Step 3 — Respect turnaround
Build in legal/contractual turnaround and meal breaks.

## Step 4 — Stress the risk
Add weather/cover and contingency days (§3 #4).

## Output
A shoot schedule grouped by location and cast, with moves, turnaround, and cover.
