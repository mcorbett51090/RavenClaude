# Senior-care KPI glossary

The metrics a senior-care operation is judged on — formulas, the misreads, and where a figure needs a `[unverified]` mark.

## Census metrics

Occupancy, move-in/move-out rate, length of stay, inquiry-to-move-in conversion, time-to-move-in. Census is a flow (§3 #1, #7).

## Labor metrics

Acuity-based hours-per-resident-day (PPD), labor % of revenue, agency-labor share, caregiver turnover (§3 #3, #6).

## Quality metrics

Survey deficiency count, incident/fall rate, quality measures, acuity mix (§3 #4).

## Sourcing note

Figures in this file are from the author's domain knowledge and are marked `[unverified — training knowledge]` or `[ESTIMATE]` at point of use. Validate against a primary source before putting any figure in a client deliverable (§3 cite-or-mark rule).
