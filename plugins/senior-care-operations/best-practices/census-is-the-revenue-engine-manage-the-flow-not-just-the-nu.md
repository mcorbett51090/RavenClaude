# Census is the revenue engine — manage the flow, not just the number

**Status:** Absolute rule. **Constitution:** §3, §4.

## Use when
Any senior care operations deliverable where this question is in play.

## The rule
Occupancy times rate is the revenue; but census is a flow of move-ins, move-outs, and length of stay, and managing the flow (referrals, conversions, attrition) beats chasing a point-in-time number. [unverified — training knowledge]

## Why
This is house opinion §3 #1. Getting it wrong is the most common way a senior care operations analysis misleads the operator who acts on it.

## How to apply
- Apply it before reaching for a method.
- Make the relevant number carry a definition, window, and baseline (§3 #1).
- Cite the source + date for any external figure, or mark it `[unverified — training knowledge]`.
