# Quality and compliance are the license and the reputation — track them

**Status:** Pattern. **Constitution:** §3, §4.

## Use when
Any senior care operations deliverable where this question is in play.

## The rule
Survey deficiencies, incidents/falls, and quality measures are existential risk; a quality problem closes a building and ends referrals — treat them as first-class operational metrics.

## Why
This is house opinion §3 #4. Getting it wrong is the most common way a senior care operations analysis misleads the operator who acts on it.

## How to apply
- Apply it before reaching for a method.
- Make the relevant number carry a definition, window, and baseline (§3 #1).
- Cite the source + date for any external figure, or mark it `[unverified — training knowledge]`.
