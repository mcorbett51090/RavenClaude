# Price to acuity, not a flat rate

**Status:** Absolute rule. **Constitution:** §3, §4.

## Use when
Any senior care operations deliverable where this question is in play.

## The rule
Care needs vary enormously across residents; a flat rate under-charges high-acuity residents and over-charges low — an acuity-based rate captures the care cost and protects margin.

## Why
This is house opinion §3 #2. Getting it wrong is the most common way a senior care operations analysis misleads the operator who acts on it.

## How to apply
- Apply it before reaching for a method.
- Make the relevant number carry a definition, window, and baseline (§3 #1).
- Cite the source + date for any external figure, or mark it `[unverified — training knowledge]`.
