# Skill: Manage census flow

Managing the flow beats chasing a point-in-time number.

## Step 1 — Decompose the flow
Move-ins, move-outs, and length of stay (§3 #1).

## Step 2 — Locate the problem
Move-out attrition vs move-in/conversion shortfall.

## Step 3 — Read the funnel
Inquiry → tour → move-in conversion and timing (§3 #7).

## Step 4 — Act on the lever
Map the fix to the flow component.

## Output
A census-flow decomposition, the problem located, a funnel read, and the matched lever.
