# Skill: Read quality and compliance

A quality problem closes a building and ends referrals.

## Step 1 — Read survey readiness
Documentation, incidents, and quality-measure status (§3 #4).

## Step 2 — Find the incident pattern
Falls/incidents by time, location, and resident factor.

## Step 3 — Tie to operations
Map the pattern to staffing, training, or environment.

## Step 4 — Route the clinical
Send clinical/regulatory determinations to the qualified authority.

## Output
A survey-readiness read, an incident pattern, the operational response, and routed clinical items — as decision-support.
