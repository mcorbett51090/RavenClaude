# Skill: Quantify labor and turnover

Turnover is margin and quality, not HR overhead.

## Step 1 — Read labor cost
Labor as % of revenue and agency-labor share (§3 #6).

## Step 2 — Measure turnover
Caregiver turnover, benchmarked/marked (§3 #8).

## Step 3 — Cost the turnover
Recruiting, training, and agency-backfill cost.

## Step 4 — Model retention ROI
Compare a retention investment to the turnover cost.

## Output
A labor-cost read, a turnover rate, the turnover cost, and the retention ROI.
