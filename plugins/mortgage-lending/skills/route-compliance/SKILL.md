# Skill: Route compliance to counsel

A compliance determination rendered in-team is an existential risk (§3 #6).

## Step 1 — Map the workflow
Operational compliance steps in the origination cycle (disclosure timing, etc.).

## Step 2 — Read the QC signal
QC/defect rate as an operational quality signal, not a verdict (§3 #6).

## Step 3 — Separate operational from regulatory
Workflow gaps the team can flag vs determinations only counsel renders (§3 #6 #8).

## Step 4 — Route the determination
Every TRID/ECOA/HMDA/fair-lending/UDAAP determination to counsel — never in-team (§2).

## Output
An operational compliance/QC read with every regulatory determination routed to counsel. Traverse Tree 3 in the decision-trees file.
