# Stress the debt and refinance wall

## Steps (traverse top-to-bottom; do not skip)
1. Size to a DSCR and LTV — Set the loan against in-place NOI at a target DSCR and LTV; record the constraint that binds.
2. Schedule DSCR through the hold — Project DSCR each year against the NOI plan; a dip below covenant is a flag, not a footnote (§3 #6).
3. Find the refi wall — Identify the maturity year and the take-out assumption — the rate and cap at which the refinance fails.
4. Quantify equity at risk — State the equity exposed if the refi clears at a stressed rate, not the base.
