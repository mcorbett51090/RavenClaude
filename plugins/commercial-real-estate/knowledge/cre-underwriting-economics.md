# CRE underwriting economics

How a deal pencils — in-place vs pro-forma, the spread, opex and recoveries, and debt as the swing factor.

## In-place vs pro-forma

Anchor on contractual income; treat every step-up as a sourced, probability-weighted assumption (§3 #1).

## Operating expenses & recoveries

Build opex bottom-up; reimbursements and the recovery ratio drive NOI as much as rent (§3 #7).

## Debt & the refinance wall

DSCR, debt yield, maturity, and the take-out assumption — most deals fail at the refi in a high-rate environment (§3 #6).
