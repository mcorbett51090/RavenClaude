# CRE KPI & underwriting glossary

The metrics a CRE deal and an owned asset are judged on — with formulas, the common misreads, and where a benchmark needs a date.

## Return metrics

Going-in cap rate (NOI ÷ price), levered/unlevered IRR, equity multiple, cash-on-cash, and yield-on-cost. The cap rate and IRR answer different questions — see CLAUDE.md §3 #2.

## Income & lease metrics

In-place NOI, net effective rent (face minus TI/free-rent/LCs), expense recovery ratio, occupancy vs leased %, WALT (weighted average lease term).

## Risk metrics

Cap-rate-vs-Treasury spread, DSCR, debt yield, LTV, and the refinance/maturity schedule. The spread and DSCR are the two that most often decide a deal.
