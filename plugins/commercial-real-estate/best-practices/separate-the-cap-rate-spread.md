# Always separate the cap-rate-vs-Treasury spread

**Status:** Absolute rule. **Constitution:** §3, §4.

## Use when
Any time a cap rate enters a memo.

## The rule
Report the cap rate as a risk premium over the 10-yr Treasury, with its historical percentile and a date.

## Why
A cap rate alone hides whether you're paid for risk. A thin spread (e.g. ~172 bps, 24th percentile in Q3 2025) is a material risk fact.

## How to apply
- Compute cap minus the current 10-yr Treasury.
- Place the spread historically.
- Attribute any move to the rate vs the premium leg.
