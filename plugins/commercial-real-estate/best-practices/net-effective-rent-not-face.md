# Compare net effective rent, never face

**Status:** Pattern. **Constitution:** §3, §4.

## Use when
Any rent comp or lease-economics question.

## The rule
Net TI, free rent, and leasing commissions out of face rent before comparing comps.

## Why
Face rent is marketing; net effective is what the landlord earns. A face-to-NER comparison overstates competitiveness.

## How to apply
- Gather the full concession package per comp.
- Spread concessions over the term.
- Compare NER to NER only; flag credit and term.
