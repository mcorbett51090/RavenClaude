# Stress the debt and refinance wall

**Status:** Absolute rule. **Constitution:** §3, §4.

## Use when
Before any levered return is quoted.

## The rule
Schedule DSCR through the hold and surface the refinance year and the rate at which the deal breaks.

## Why
Most deals fail at the refi, not the entry. The break rate and equity-at-risk belong in the headline, not a footnote.

## How to apply
- Size to a binding DSCR/LTV/debt-yield constraint.
- Project DSCR each year.
- State the equity at risk if the refi clears at a stressed rate.
