# Skill: Stress the debt and refinance wall

Most deals die at the refi, not the entry. This makes the debt and the refinance wall explicit.

## Step 1 — Size to a DSCR and LTV
Set the loan against in-place NOI at a target DSCR and LTV; record the constraint that binds.

## Step 2 — Schedule DSCR through the hold
Project DSCR each year against the NOI plan; a dip below covenant is a flag, not a footnote (§3 #6).

## Step 3 — Find the refi wall
Identify the maturity year and the take-out assumption — the rate and cap at which the refinance fails.

## Step 4 — Quantify equity at risk
State the equity exposed if the refi clears at a stressed rate, not the base.

## Output
A debt schedule, a DSCR path, the refi year and break rate, and the equity at risk under stress.
