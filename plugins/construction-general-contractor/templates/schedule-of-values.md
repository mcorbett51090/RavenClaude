# Schedule of Values Template

> **Usage:** copy to `docs/contracts/` or `docs/billing/` in your project directory.
> Complete before submitting the first pay application. This template conforms to the
> AIA G703 format; adapt column headers to match owner requirements (Textura, Procore
> Pay Apps, etc.). All dollar values must sum to the contract amount.

## Stored Materials

Stored materials may be billed on the SOV only if:
- [ ] Stored on-site or in a bonded warehouse per contract terms
- [ ] Insured under the GC's builder's risk policy
- [ ] Supporting documentation (invoice, bill of lading, warehouse receipt) attached to pay app
- [ ] Owner/architect approved in advance (some contracts require written approval)

## Lien Waiver Checklist (attach to each pay application)

- [ ] GC conditional waiver for current period amount
- [ ] GC unconditional waiver for prior period amount
- [ ] Sub conditional waivers for current period (all subs billing this period)
- [ ] Sub unconditional waivers for prior period
- [ ] Supplier / vendor waivers where required by contract
