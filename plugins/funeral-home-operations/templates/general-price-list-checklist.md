## 1. General Price List (GPL)
- ☐ **Itemized** — individual prices for each good and service offered (not only packages)
- ☐ Given **to keep** to anyone who asks in person about arrangements or prices
- ☐ Given **at the start** of the in-person arrangement discussion
- ☐ Includes the required **disclosure statements** (verify current Rule text):
  - ☐ Right to select only the items desired (**no forced packages** — no item the law lets a family decline is a condition of buying another)
  - ☐ **Embalming-not-required** disclosure (embalming rarely legally required; refrigeration / closed-timeline alternative)
  - ☐ Basic-services-fee disclosure
  - ☐ Cash-advance-items disclosure (third-party items and any mark-up handling)
  - ☐ Casket / outer-burial-container price-list availability note

## 2. Casket Price List (CPL)
- ☐ Prices for the caskets offered, shown/available **before** the family views caskets
- ☐ Includes alternative containers (for cremation) where offered
- ☐ No representation that a casket preserves the body indefinitely (**no misrepresentation**)

## 3. Outer Burial Container Price List
- ☐ Prices for outer burial containers / vaults, shown **before** viewing
- ☐ States that an **outer burial container is generally not required for cremation** (don't imply otherwise)
- ☐ Notes that any vault requirement is set by the **cemetery**, not the funeral home

## 4. Telephone price disclosure
- ☐ Price and terms questions are **answered on the phone**
- ☐ Callers are told the GPL / CPL / OBC lists exist and are available

## 5. No-misrepresentation review (across all materials)
- ☐ No false claim that a law requires a purchase (embalming, casket, vault) when it doesn't
- ☐ No false preservation / protective-value claims for embalming, caskets, or containers
- ☐ Cash-advance items presented honestly (what's a pass-through vs a service)
- ☐ Cremation presented accurately (no implied required container/vault)

## 6. Itemized statement of goods & services selected
- ☐ Provided to the family **after** selections are made
- ☐ Lists each item selected, its price, the total, and cash-advance items
- ☐ Notes any legal-requirement basis where an item is genuinely required (with the source)

## 7. Disposition-specific check (does the list serve every disposition offered?)
- ☐ Burial — casket, outer burial container, embalming disclosures present
- ☐ Cremation — alternative container / direct-cremation option; no implied vault requirement
- ☐ Alkaline hydrolysis / aquamation — offered? legal in this state? (**verify**) priced?
- ☐ Green / natural burial — shroud / biodegradable casket / no-vault option where offered
