# Engineer payments; route accounting and regulation out

This team builds the ledger, the PSP integration, and the billing system and emits clean money events — but revenue recognition (ASC 606) and general-ledger posting route to finance, financial-services regulation (money transmission, AML/KYC, licensing) routes to regulatory-compliance, and the security/PCI verdict routes to ravenclaude-core/security-reviewer. Building the mechanism is in scope; rendering the accounting treatment, the regulatory determination, and the formal sign-off is not.
