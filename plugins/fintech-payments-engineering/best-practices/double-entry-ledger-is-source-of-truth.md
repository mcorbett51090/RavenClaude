# Keep a double-entry ledger as the source of truth

Record every money movement as balanced debits and credits in an append-only ledger you own, and treat the payment service provider as an integration you continuously reconcile against — not as your accounting record. The PSP can be wrong, delayed, or disputed; an owned double-entry ledger gives you an auditable, internally-consistent source of truth, and continuous reconciliation turns discrepancies into caught bugs or fraud signals instead of mystery money.
