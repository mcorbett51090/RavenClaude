## 6. Meeting / discovery call agenda (when booked)

**Goal of the call:** Qualify the opportunity, confirm the trigger, and agree a next step (quote request or RFQ timeline).

1. Confirm their current routing and pain point on [primary lane] (5 min)
2. Share the value hypothesis and test it with them (5 min)
3. Qualification questions — volume, timing, decision process, incumbent contract expiry (5 min)
4. Agree next step — quote, site visit, or include in upcoming tender (5 min)

**Qualification questions:**
- "What's your current transit time commitment on [LANE] and how often does it slip?"
- "When does your current forwarder contract come up for review?"
- "Who else is involved in the decision — logistics, procurement, finance?"
- "What would a better outcome look like in 6 months?"
