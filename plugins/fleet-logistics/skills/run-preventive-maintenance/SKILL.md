# Skill: Run preventive maintenance to CPM

PM is cheaper than the breakdown.

## Step 1 — Read maintenance CPM
Maintenance cost ÷ miles, by truck and age (§3 #5).

## Step 2 — Measure unplanned downtime
Separate planned PM from unplanned failures.

## Step 3 — Tighten the PM schedule
Set PM intervals that beat the failure curve.

## Step 4 — Call replacement on lifecycle
Replace when rising maintenance CPM exceeds new-iron cost.

## Output
A maintenance-CPM read, a downtime split, a PM schedule, and a lifecycle replacement call.
