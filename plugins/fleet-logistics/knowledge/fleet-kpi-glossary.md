# Fleet KPI glossary

The metrics a fleet is judged on — formulas, the misreads, and where a benchmark needs a date.

## Cost metrics

Cost-per-mile (all-in ~$2.26, non-fuel marginal ~$1.78), operating ratio, maintenance CPM, fuel CPM/MPG. CPM and OR are the master numbers (§3 #1, #2).

## Utilization metrics

Deadhead/empty-mile %, loaded-mile ratio, revenue-per-truck-per-day, truck utilization (§3 #3).

## People & asset metrics

Driver turnover (often 90%+ at large TL), unplanned-downtime rate, replacement-cycle cost (§3 #4, #5).
