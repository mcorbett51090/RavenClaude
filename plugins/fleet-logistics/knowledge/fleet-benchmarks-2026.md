# Fleet & trucking benchmarks (2024–2026)

Dated benchmarks for a fleet read. ATRI updates annually — every figure carries a source and a retrieval date.

## Cost per mile

Average all-in operating cost ~$2.260/mile in 2024 (down 0.4% YoY); excluding fuel, marginal costs rose 3.6% to a record ~$1.779/mile. Average dry-van spot rate ~$2.03/mile (§3 #1).

## Operating margin

For-hire truckload operating margin fell to ~-2.3% in 2024 — the first loss since 2019 (§3 #2).

## Driver turnover

Private-carrier turnover ~18.4%; for-hire turnover is almost always over 30% and regularly exceeds 50%, with major truckload carriers often surpassing 90% (§3 #4).
