# Driver turnover is a unit-economics problem, not HR overhead

**Status:** Pattern. **Constitution:** §3, §4.

## Use when
Any fleet & logistics deliverable where this question is in play.

## The rule
Turnover often exceeds 90% at large truckload carriers; each replacement carries recruiting, training, and unseated-truck cost — retention is a margin lever.

## Why
This is house opinion §3 #4. Getting it wrong is the most common way a fleet & logistics analysis misleads the operator who acts on it.

## How to apply
- Apply it before reaching for a method.
- Make the relevant number carry a definition, window, and baseline (§3 #1).
- Cite the source + date for any external figure, or mark it `[unverified — training knowledge]`.
