# Deadhead and utilization are the revenue leaks

**Status:** Pattern. **Constitution:** §3, §4.

## Use when
Any fleet & logistics deliverable where this question is in play.

## The rule
Empty miles and idle trucks are unpriced cost; revenue-per-truck-per-day and loaded-mile ratio expose what the rate-per-mile hides.

## Why
This is house opinion §3 #3. Getting it wrong is the most common way a fleet & logistics analysis misleads the operator who acts on it.

## How to apply
- Apply it before reaching for a method.
- Make the relevant number carry a definition, window, and baseline (§3 #1).
- Cite the source + date for any external figure, or mark it `[unverified — training knowledge]`.
