## [0.1.0] — 2026-06-08

Initial release.

- **4 agents** — `pharmacy-operations-lead`, `fill-workflow-analyst`, `inventory-reimbursement-specialist`, `adherence-clinical-specialist`, each carrying the full scenario-authoring schema.
- **5 skills + 5 commands** — `size-throughput-staffing`, `compute-real-margin`, `balance-inventory`, `translate-adherence`, `protect-dispensing-safety`.
- **4-file knowledge bank** — KPI glossary, unit economics, 2025–2026 context, and Mermaid decision trees.
- **`scripts/pharmacy_operations_calc.py`** — stdlib calculator: `throughput-staffing`, `margin`, `adherence`. Decision-support only.
- **4 templates · 8 best-practice rules · scenarios bank · 1 advisory hook**.
