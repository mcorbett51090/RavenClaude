# Skill: Diagnose funnel

More leads into a leaking funnel wastes spend (§3 #3).

## Step 1 — Map the stages
Lead → qual → demo → proposal → close with conversion + dwell each.

## Step 2 — Find the leak
Lowest conversion or longest dwell via `revops_calc.py funnel` (§3 #3).

## Step 3 — Localize the cause
Qualification, process, or comp behind the leaking stage.

## Step 4 — Fix the constraint first
Then add volume — not before (§3 #3).

## Output
A stage-by-stage funnel read naming the leaking stage and the fix. Traverse Tree 2 in the decision-trees file.
