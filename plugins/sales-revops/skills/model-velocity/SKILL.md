# Skill: Model velocity

Velocity has four levers; moving one often moves another (§3 #3).

## Step 1 — Establish the baseline
Current deals, win-rate, ACV, cycle-length.

## Step 2 — Compute velocity
(deals × win-rate × ACV) ÷ cycle via `revops_calc.py velocity`.

## Step 3 — Test each lever
Which single lever moves the number most, and its side-effect.

## Step 4 — Pick the realistic lever
Tied to the funnel diagnosis, not a wish.

## Output
A velocity model showing the moving lever and its trade-offs.
