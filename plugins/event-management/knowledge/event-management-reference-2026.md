## Registration & ticketing platforms

| Tool | Use for | Note |
|---|---|---|
| **Eventbrite** | Public/community ticketing, broad discovery | Consumer-grade; fees vary by plan `[verify-at-use]` |
| **Cvent** | Large/complex corporate events, end-to-end | Enterprise; registration + venue sourcing + badging |
| **RegFox / Ti.to** | Mid-market custom registration forms | Flexible forms, lower fees than consumer platforms `[verify-at-use]` |
| **Luma** | Lightweight community + recurring events | Fast setup; good for meetups/webinars |

## Virtual & hybrid platforms

| Tool | Use for | Note |
|---|---|---|
| **Zoom Webinars / Events** | Webinars, broadcast-style virtual | Ubiquitous; attendee limits by plan `[verify-at-use]` |
| **Hopin / RingCentral Events** | Multi-track virtual & hybrid | Check current product status before quoting `[verify-at-use]` |
| **Airmeet / Bizzabo** | Engagement-heavy virtual/hybrid | Networking, expo booths, sponsor surfaces |
| **StreamYard / Restream** | Producing the live stream itself | Multistreaming, branded studio |

## Benchmark rules of thumb — `[verify-at-use against YOUR data]`

> These are starting priors only. Your own historical actuals beat any industry average — replace these as soon as you have data.

| Metric | Rough prior | Caveat |
|---|---|---|
| Free virtual no-show rate | ~40-60% register-to-attend drop | Highly variable; free events no-show much more `[verify-at-use]` |
| Paid in-person attrition | much lower than free virtual | Skin-in-the-game effect; depends on price/audience |
| Budget contingency line | 10-20% of total | Higher for first-time or complex/hybrid events |
| Landing-page register conversion | low single-digit to teens % | Depends entirely on traffic quality + offer `[verify-at-use]` |
| Sponsorship as share of revenue | varies widely by event type | B2B conferences lean heavily on it; community events less |
