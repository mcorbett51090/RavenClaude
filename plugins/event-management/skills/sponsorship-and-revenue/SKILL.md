# Sponsorship & Revenue

Sponsorship is a **fulfilled promise**, not a logo on a slide. Sell value, deliver value, prove value.

## Tiering by value

| Tier | Sells (deliverables) | Fulfillment proof |
|---|---|---|
| Title / Platinum | Top placement, keynote/speaking slot, lead list, branded moment | Photos, lead CSV, recording, placement screenshots |
| Gold | Booth, session, logo on key surfaces, lead scan | Booth photo, scan export, surface audit |
| Silver / Community | Logo, mention, attendee list opt-ins | Surface audit, mention log |

## The three steps
1. **Sell the value.** A prospectus that states what each tier *delivers*, not just where the logo goes. Price against goal-relevant value (audience, leads, exposure).
2. **Deliver the value.** A fulfillment checklist per sponsor — every promised deliverable, with an owner and a due date relative to the event.
3. **Prove the value.** Capture evidence of each deliverable (leads, placement, attendance) — the renewal conversation runs on proof.

## Anti-patterns
- Sponsorship sold as "a logo" with no defined deliverables.
- Promises sold and never fulfilled (the fastest way to lose a sponsor).
- No proof captured, so renewal is a guess.
