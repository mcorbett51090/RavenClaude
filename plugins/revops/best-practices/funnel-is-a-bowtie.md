# The funnel is a bowtie, not a triangle

B2B revenue compounds after closed-won, so model acquisition and retention/expansion as one connected motion — a bowtie — not a one-way triangle that ends at the close. A pipeline view that stops at closed-won under-models the business and ignores where net-revenue-retention actually comes from. The bowtie's right side (onboarding → adoption → renewal → expansion) hands the health/churn model to `customer-success-analytics`, but the funnel definition must connect the two sides as one revenue engine.
