# Coverage is derived from win-rate, not a folk constant

Required pipeline coverage = gap-to-target ÷ this segment's historical stage-weighted win-rate — not a hand-me-down '3x'. A 3x rule is somebody else's win-rate (33%) applied to your business; if your segment converts at 20% you need 5x and a 3x target will miss, while if you convert at 50% you're padding to a myth at 2x. Derive the target from *this* segment's conversion, recompute it as win-rates drift, and only ever apply it to inspected (not padded) pipeline.
