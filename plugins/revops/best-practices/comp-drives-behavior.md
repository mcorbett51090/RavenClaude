# Comp drives behavior — model the incentive, not just the math

Every comp/quota/territory design is a behavior change before it is a spreadsheet, so name what it rewards *and* what it accidentally rewards before it ships. A cap invites end-of-period deal-holding and dumping; a pure-new-logo plan starves expansion; no clawback rewards bad-fit closes that churn next quarter; a steep accelerator can make a rep sandbag a deal into next period to clear quota first. Write down the intended behavior, then red-team the plan for the perverse one — the plan that looks fairest on paper is often the one whose loophole reps find first. Pair OTE/pay-mix, accelerators, caps, and clawback as one system, and re-read them as "what would a rational rep do to maximize this" rather than "what do we wish they'd do."
