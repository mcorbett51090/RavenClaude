# Inspect the pipeline before the pipeline math

Coverage ratios, win-rates, and sales velocity computed on stale or padded pipeline are precise nonsense. Before trusting any aggregate, inspect for stuck/aged deals, deals past their close date, opportunities with no recent activity, and amounts that don't reconcile — then compute on the cleaned number. Padded pipeline is worse than thin pipeline: thin pipeline you can see, but padded pipeline lies inside the aggregate. Every rolled-up number should be traceable to an inspected deal list, not a raw CRM sum.
