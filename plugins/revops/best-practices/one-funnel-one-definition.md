# One funnel, one definition, one source of truth

MQL/SQL/SAL/opportunity-stage mean exactly one thing across marketing, sales, and customer success — defined once, instrumented once, and reported from one source of truth. Two teams running two definitions of the same stage is the root cause of most RevOps disputes: marketing 'hits MQL target' while sales calls the leads garbage. Agree the definition (by criteria the downstream team accepts), wire an accept/reject feedback loop, and measure each stage at exactly one instrumentation point.
