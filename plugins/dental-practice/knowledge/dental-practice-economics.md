# Dental practice economics

How a practice makes money — overhead, collections, case acceptance, production, and payer mix.

## Overhead & collections

Overhead ~62% median; collections target 98%+ — the two master margin numbers (§3 #1, #2).

## Acceptance & production

Case acceptance is presentation; production per hour and hygiene are the capacity engines (§3 #3, #4, #5).

## Payer mix

PPO write-offs set the effective fee — manage the mix deliberately (§3 #6).
