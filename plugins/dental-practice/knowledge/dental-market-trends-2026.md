# Dental market & benchmarks (2025–2026)

Dated benchmarks for a practice read. Costs moved with labor and payers — every figure carries a source and a retrieval date.

## Overhead & wages

National median overhead ~62% of collections; high performers 55–60%. Wages now ~25–30% of collections; 2025 pressures: rising supply/labor costs and slower reimbursements (§3 #1).

## Production & collections

Dentist production ~$475–$575/hr (high performers $700+); hygiene ~$145–$175/hr. Target collection % ≥98%; profit margins typically 30–40% (§3 #2, #4).

## Consolidation

Group/DSO locations often run 28–35% margins on shared overhead — the position context for an independent (§3 #7).
