# Dental practice KPI glossary

The metrics a dental practice is judged on — formulas, the misreads, and where a benchmark needs a date.

## Revenue & collection metrics

Production, collections, collection ratio (target 98%+), A/R aging, effective fee by payer. Collections pay the bills, not production (§3 #2).

## Production & capacity metrics

Doctor and hygiene production per hour, schedule utilization, hygiene reappointment, case acceptance.

## Margin metrics

Overhead % (median ~62%), staff cost % (~25–30%), net margin, and the DSO-margin context (§3 #1, #7).
