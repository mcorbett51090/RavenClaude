# Skill: Lift case acceptance

Acceptance is communication, not price.

## Step 1 — Measure acceptance by plan type
Track accepted vs presented for hygiene, restorative, and major (§3 #3).

## Step 2 — Find the drop-off
Identify where the big plans fail — presentation, sequencing, or financial options.

## Step 3 — Re-sequence and present
Phase the plan and present it as decision-support with financial options.

## Step 4 — Re-measure
Track acceptance before and after.

## Output
An acceptance read by plan type, the drop-off, a presentation/sequencing fix, and the before/after.
