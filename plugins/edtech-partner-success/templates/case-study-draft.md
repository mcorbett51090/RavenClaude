> Example (fully-anonymous): *"A mid-sized K-12 district in the Pacific Northwest lifted family-engagement response rates 41% in 12 months."*

### 2. Context (2-3 sentences)

- Partner segment: `<K-12 / higher-ed / corporate L&D>`
- Size + scope: `<district enrollment / institution FTE / employee count>`

### 3. The challenge (2-3 short paragraphs)

What was broken / missing / underperforming. Quote the partner's words when possible (attributed to a role, not necessarily a name, depending on anonymization).

### 4. What we did (3-5 bullet points)

Specific, mechanical, the next consultant could replicate it. Avoid marketing puffery ("we delivered transformative engagement"); favor verbs that the partner's CIO would recognize.

## Pre-publication checklist

- [ ] **Anonymization choice confirmed** by partner (header)
- [ ] **All numeric claims sourced** (no "engagement went up dramatically" — every number cites a data range)
- [ ] **No student/parent name** unless parental consent collected AND on file
- [ ] **No student PII** beyond directory-info (per district designation)
- [ ] **District legal review** complete (timeline: 2-8 weeks depending on state; CA / NY / IL typically slower)
- [ ] **Named-individual sign-off** for every quote (role + name)
- [ ] **State media-release rule** checked (some states require additional sign-off for any quote attributed to district personnel)
- [ ] **Sub-processor disclosures** still accurate (case study referencing vendor X must match current sub-processor list)
- [ ] **AI-feature claims** consistent with COPPA-amended consent posture (post April 22, 2026)
