# Renewal decision memo — `<partner_name>`

> **What this is.** The PSM-authored decision memo that goes into a partner's renewal recommendation. Distinct from the renewal *play* (which the PSM executes) — this is the *artifact* that names the recommendation, the evidence, and the open risks for leadership / cross-functional review.
>
> **When to fill in.** 120-180 days before renewal date for K-12 (per the [K-12 clock](../knowledge/renewal-pricing-conversations-edtech.md)); 90 days for higher-ed and corp L&D. Land it BEFORE the partner-facing renewal conversation, not after.
>
> **Audience.** PSM leadership + AE + (sometimes) Finance / Legal / Product depending on the recommendation.
>
> **Pair with:** [`success-plan.md`](success-plan.md), [`partner-profile.md`](partner-profile.md), [`../knowledge/renewal-pricing-conversations-edtech.md`](../knowledge/renewal-pricing-conversations-edtech.md), [`cross-functional-partnership-map.md`](cross-functional-partnership-map.md).
