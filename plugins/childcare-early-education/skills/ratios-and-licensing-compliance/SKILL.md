# Ratios & Licensing Compliance

Compliance is a **resting state maintained daily**, not a scramble before an inspection. Everything here is **state-specific and `[verify-at-use]`** — confirm against your current licensing regulation before it drives a decision.

## The loop

1. **Read ratio and group size as two separate limits.** The child:staff ratio caps how many children one qualified adult may supervise; the group-size cap limits total children in a room regardless of staff. A room can satisfy one and violate the other. Check both, by age, for every configuration — see the staff-a-room-to-ratio tree in [`../../knowledge/childcare-decision-trees.md`](../../knowledge/childcare-decision-trees.md).
2. **Confirm who counts toward ratio.** Qualifications, background checks, and required training determine which adults are ratio-countable. An adult present but not qualified does not fix an out-of-ratio room `[verify-at-use, state-specific]`.
3. **Maintain the licensing domains daily.** Ratios, staff qualifications and files, health and safety, records/documentation, and physical environment are the domains a licensor inspects — treat each as a daily-maintained system, not an inspection-day project (licensing-readiness tree).
4. **Document incidents and health-and-safety at the standard.** Contemporaneous records (what, when, who, signatures, retention) are what survive an inspection and protect children, staff, and the license.
5. **Hand the cost consequence off.** The staffing level a ratio forces is a cost the tuition must cover — that economics call is `childcare-center-lead`'s; see [`../staffing-to-ratio-scheduling/SKILL.md`](../staffing-to-ratio-scheduling/SKILL.md).

## Metrics

| Metric | Reads | Note |
|---|---|---|
| Moments-out-of-ratio | any interval below required ratio | Target zero — a moment out is a citation risk |
| Group-size compliance | rooms within the age cap | Independent of ratio; check separately |
| Staff-file completeness | qualified/verified files current | Gaps here can void ratio-countable headcount |
| Licensing-domain readiness | domains audit-clean | Continuous, not pre-visit |

## Anti-patterns

- Averaging ratio across a day instead of holding it every moment.
- Checking ratio but not the separate group-size cap (or vice versa).
- Counting an unqualified/unverified adult toward ratio.
- "Getting ready" for a licensing visit — i.e. being non-compliant between visits.
