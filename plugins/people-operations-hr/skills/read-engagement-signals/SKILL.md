# Skill: Read engagement signals

Engagement is a leading indicator of attrition — read it segmented, not company-wide (§3 #4).

## Step 1 — Segment first
Break eNPS / favorability by team, tenure cohort, and manager before reading any company number (§3 #4).

## Step 2 — Find the at-risk pocket
Localize the low-favorability, high-attrition-risk segment; a company average hides it (§3 #4, #7).

## Step 3 — Tie to attrition risk
Cross the engagement pocket against regretted-attrition trend — engagement leads exits.

## Step 4 — Isolate manager quality
Team-level engagement and attrition deltas separate the manager/span effect from role and level (§3 #7).

## Output
A segmented engagement read naming the at-risk pockets, the manager/span effects, and the forward attrition risk — with owners/dates on each action. See [`../../knowledge/people-ops-kpi-glossary.md`](../../knowledge/people-ops-kpi-glossary.md).
