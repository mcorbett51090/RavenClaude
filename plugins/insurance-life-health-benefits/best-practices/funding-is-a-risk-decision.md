# Funding is a risk decision, not a price tag

Fully-insured buys predictability for a higher fixed premium; self-funded keeps the risk and the upside but needs stop-loss (specific + aggregate), cash-flow tolerance, and a group large enough to absorb claims variance; level-funded is the middle path. Size the choice to the group's risk profile, never to a vendor's pitch — and never self-fund just to dodge one bad renewal. Self-funding is a multi-year posture; a group that can't absorb a bad claims year should not take the risk.
