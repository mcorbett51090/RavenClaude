# Decompose every renewal into its parts

A renewal is a sum of parts, not a single number. Decompose every increase into trend, the group's own experience, pooling / large-claim adjustments, demographic drift, and plan/benefit changes. "+22%" is not a finding; "trend +7, adverse experience +9, demographic +3, plan-buy-up +3" is. If a carrier won't show how it built the increase, that opacity is itself a reason to test the market — a defensible renewal can be explained.
