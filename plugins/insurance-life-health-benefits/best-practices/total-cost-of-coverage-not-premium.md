# Optimize total cost of coverage, not just premium

The cheapest premium can be the most expensive plan once deductibles, coinsurance, and out-of-pocket maximums hit the workforce. Optimize the *member's* total cost exposure against the employer's budget, not the headline rate. A low-premium HDHP with no employer HSA contribution is a cost-shift dressed as a benefit — model what members actually pay across a realistic claims distribution before recommending it.
