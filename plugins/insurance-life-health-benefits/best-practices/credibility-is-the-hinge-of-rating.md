# Credibility is the hinge of rating

Whether a group's own claims experience should drive its rate is a function of group size and claim volume. Small groups lean on manual/pooled rates; large groups on their own experience; most groups are a credibility-weighted blend. Name the credibility weight before arguing about the rate. A single large claim in a low-credibility group is mostly noise — pooling exists for exactly that — so don't blow up the program over a non-credible spike, and don't ignore credible experience on a large group.
