# Precision-ag KPI glossary

The metrics a farm operation is judged on — formulas, the misreads, and where a figure needs a date or `[unverified]`.

## Yield & input metrics

Yield by management zone, input economic optimum, input ROI, variable-rate coverage. Read yield by zone, not field average (§3 #1, #2).

## Economic metrics

Cost per acre, margin per acre by field, breakeven yield/price, land cost share (§3 #4).

## Agronomic metrics

Soil/tissue levels, crop-removal rates, scout-threshold ratio, operation-timing adherence (§3 #5, #6).

## Sourcing note

Figures in this file are from the author's domain knowledge and are marked `[unverified — training knowledge]` or `[ESTIMATE]` at point of use. Validate against a primary source before putting any figure in a client deliverable (§3 cite-or-mark rule).
