# Skill: Build per-acre economics

A whole-farm average buries the field that's losing money.

## Step 1 — Cost per acre by field
Inputs, operations, land, and overhead per acre (§3 #4).

## Step 2 — Margin per acre by field
Revenue (yield × price) minus cost, by field.

## Step 3 — Rank the fields
Identify the profitable and the loss-making acres.

## Step 4 — Act
Map input/rotation/marketing changes to the laggard fields.

## Output
A per-acre cost-and-margin read by field with the laggards identified.
