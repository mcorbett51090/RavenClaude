# Skill: Manage by zone

A field average hides the productive and the problem zones.

## Step 1 — Delineate zones
Build management zones from yield, soil, and topography (§3 #2).

## Step 2 — Read yield by zone
Compare zone yields, not the field mean.

## Step 3 — Vary the inputs
Put inputs where they return; pull them where they don't.

## Step 4 — Measure the lift
Track per-zone return vs uniform application.

## Output
Delineated zones, a per-zone yield read, a variable-rate plan, and the return lift.
