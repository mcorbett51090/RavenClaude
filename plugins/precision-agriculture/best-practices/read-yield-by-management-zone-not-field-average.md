# Read yield by management zone, not field average

**Status:** Absolute rule. **Constitution:** §3, §4.

## Use when
Any precision agriculture deliverable where this question is in play.

## The rule
A field average hides the productive and the problem zones; variable-rate and zone management put inputs where they pay and pull them where they don't.

## Why
This is house opinion §3 #2. Getting it wrong is the most common way a precision agriculture analysis misleads the operator who acts on it.

## How to apply
- Apply it before reaching for a method.
- Make the relevant number carry a definition, window, and baseline (§3 #1).
- Cite the source + date for any external figure, or mark it `[unverified — training knowledge]`.
