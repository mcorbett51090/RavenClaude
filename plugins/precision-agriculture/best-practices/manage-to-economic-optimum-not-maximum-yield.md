# Manage to economic optimum, not maximum yield

**Status:** Absolute rule. **Constitution:** §3, §4.

## Use when
Any precision agriculture deliverable where this question is in play.

## The rule
The last bushel often costs more than it returns; input decisions are made at the economic optimum where marginal return equals marginal cost, not at agronomic maximum. [unverified — training knowledge]

## Why
This is house opinion §3 #1. Getting it wrong is the most common way a precision agriculture analysis misleads the operator who acts on it.

## How to apply
- Apply it before reaching for a method.
- Make the relevant number carry a definition, window, and baseline (§3 #1).
- Cite the source + date for any external figure, or mark it `[unverified — training knowledge]`.
