## The one correction every engagement doc should carry

**Soliant's current parent is The Vistria Group (private equity, Chicago) — NOT Adecco.** The Adecco lineage is historical and ended in 2020. Ownership chain:

| Period | Owner | Note |
|---|---|---|
| 2009–2020 | **The Adecco Group** | Acquired via the MPS Group deal (2009). |
| Jan 2020–2024 | **Olympus Partners** (PE) | Adecco divested Soliant for **$612M**. Olympus grew it ~4× and took ~$300M in dividends. |
| **Jul 2024–present** | **The Vistria Group** (PE) | Acquired for **~$2.5B (~9.6× EBITDA)**, announced Jul 29 2024; Soliant is the 14th company in Vistria's "Knowledge & Learning Solutions" portfolio. |

There is **no** Aya / Cross Country / Clipboard involvement with Soliant — those are separate firms. **Confidence: High.** Sources: [Adecco divestment 2020](https://www.adeccogroup.com/our-group/media/press-releases/2020-the-adecco-group-completes-the-divestment-of-soliant-health); [Olympus→Vistria, PRNewswire](https://www.prnewswire.com/news-releases/olympus-sells-soliant-to-move-to-the-head-of-the-class-302208218.html); [Vistria portfolio](https://vistria.com/portfolio-items/soliant/); [Scope Research, $2.5B / 9.6× EBITDA](https://www.scoperesearch.co/post/healthcare-staffing-ebitda-multiples-soliant-acquired-by-vistria-for-2-5b-9-6x-ebitda).

## 1. Overview

- **Founded** 1991 (company's own framing; CB Insights agrees). Some financial-press summaries say 1992 — minor discrepancy. **Confidence: Medium.**
- **HQ:** Peachtree Corners, GA (metro Atlanta), 5550 Peachtree Pkwy — a 58,000 sq ft HQ occupied in 2020 (earlier records list Norcross / Tucker, all metro-Atlanta). [Living in Peachtree Corners](https://livinginpeachtreecorners.com/2020/business/soliant-relocates-peachtree-corners/).
- **Scale (at the 2024 Vistria deal):** ~**14,000** outsourced professionals on assignment (up from ~3,500 in 2020); **1,200+** internal recruiters; database of **200,000+** school professionals; **33,000+** school professionals placed in 2024 across all 50 states. [corpdev.org](https://www.corpdev.org/2024/08/27/olympus-sells-soliant-health-a-2-5bn-staffing-success-story/).
- **Revenue mix:** **Education ≈ 75% of revenue and ~80% of EBITDA.** Exact current annual revenue is not public (third-party aggregators estimate ~$384M `[unverified]`); only the $2.5B valuation and segment splits are disclosed. **Confidence: High** on the split, **Low** on the revenue figure. [The Healthcare Investor](https://www.thehealthcareinvestor.com/2024/08/articles/healthcare-services-investing/healthcare-life-sciences-private-equity-deal-tracker-olympus-partners-sells-soliant-to-vistria-group/).

> **Engagement implication:** despite "Health" in the name, **the K-12 special-education book is the strategic core.** Lead the engagement there unless the client scopes you specifically to healthcare.

## 2. Divisions

### Education ("Soliant Education" / school-based) — all 50 states, the larger segment
Special-education teachers; **SLP** (+ SLPAs, CFY clinical fellows); **OT / COTA**; **PT**; **school psychologists**; **BCBA / RBT / ABA**; **school nurses**; **paraprofessionals / aides**. A distinct **teletherapy** line (speech, school psych, counseling, deaf/HoH, TVI, interpreters). Clients are K-12 public & charter districts incl. some of the largest in the US. Offers an **"Enterprise Education Staffing"** product for large districts and publishes an annual *State of Special Education Staffing Report*. Sources: [Soliant school jobs](https://www.soliant.com/school-healthcare-jobs/); [Enterprise Education Staffing](https://www.soliant.com/enterprise-education-staffing/).

### Healthcare
**Allied health** (historically a **top-3 US allied firm** per SIA — imaging/radiology, radiation oncology, respiratory, medical lab); **travel nursing** (SIA-ranked ~**13th-largest** travel-nurse firm); **physicians / locum tenens & advanced practice** (EM, FP, hospitalist, IM, peds, psych, radiology, telemedicine, NP/PA); **pharmacy**; **healthcare IT**; **telehealth**. Sources: [Soliant staffing services](https://www.soliant.com/staffing-services/); [allied health](https://www.soliant.com/allied-health-jobs/).

**Brand note:** offerings sit under the single "Soliant" / "Soliant Health" brand, not distinct sub-brands. **ProCare Therapy** appears as a *competitor* on Glassdoor comparisons, **not** a confirmed Soliant sister brand — treat as `[unverified]` / likely separate.

## 3. Service model
Travel/contract (with housing & travel), **per-diem**, and **permanent / direct-hire** — all three on both sides. **Teletherapy** is a sizeable distinct line (esp. education). Soliant operates *as a supplier within* **MSP/VMS** programs; no clear evidence it sells a proprietary MSP/VMS product `[unverified]`.

## 4. Credentialing / compliance
**Joint Commission "Gold Seal" for Health Care Staffing Services — certified since 2011.** In-house credentialing/payroll/benefits team. Reference checking automated via **RefAssured "Hire Quality."** Healthcare candidate requirements: state license per practice state, ARRT (imaging), current BCLS/CPR, physicals/vaccinations, recent experience, references. Sources: [Soliant on Joint Commission](https://www.soliant.com/blog/joint-commission/); [RefAssured](https://www.refassured.com/soliant-refassured-hire-quality/).

## 5. Reputation
- **SIA:** repeatedly among the 10 largest US healthcare staffing firms (mid-2010s); ~13th travel nursing; ~3rd allied health; one secondary source places it ~28th-largest US staffing firm overall `[unverified secondary]`.
- **Glassdoor:** ~**3.5/5** (~810 reviews), ~59% recommend. **Caution for any recruiter-ops scope:** the internal **Recruiter** role rates far lower (~1.7/5, 37 reviews) — a known high-pressure sales-recruiter culture pattern. [Glassdoor](https://www.glassdoor.com/Reviews/Soliant-Health-Reviews-E15557.htm).
- **ClearlyRated / Best of Staffing:** has a ClearlyRated profile; a *current* Best of Staffing award could not be verified `[unverified]`.

## 6. Mission / culture
- **Mission:** *"We make life better for those who make lives better."*
- **Values:** innovation/curiosity, technology-forward; a "shovel" growth/recognition motif used internally. Now under a "purpose-and-profit" PE owner (Vistria) emphasizing education-sector impact. Balance the mission-driven framing against the Glassdoor recruiter-culture signal if the engagement touches recruiter comp or operations.
