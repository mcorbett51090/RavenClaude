# Competitor landscape — healthcare + education staffing

> **Last reviewed:** 2026-06-04. Competitive-intelligence reference for the `workforce-market-analyst`, framed around a dual-segment (healthcare + education) firm of Soliant's shape. **Refresh when:** SIA publishes a new largest-firms list, or a material M&A event lands (the space consolidates fast). Sizing anchors to SIA's free editorial summary; finer placements (positions 6–89, exact revenues) sit behind the SIA paywall — flagged `[unverified]` where they come from secondary press.

**Two currency corrections to carry:**
1. **Soliant's parent is The Vistria Group** (PE, since Jul 2024) — **not Adecco** (that ended in 2020).
2. **The Aya–Cross Country merger was TERMINATED** (Dec 4 2025; FTC second request, deal lapsed, Aya paid CCRN a $20M break fee). Both remain **independent** competitors. [SEC 8-K, CCRN 2025](https://www.sec.gov/Archives/edgar/data/0001141103/000095010325015716/dp238334_8k.htm).

## 3. Positioning — where Soliant wins vs. where competitors lead

**Dual-segment (healthcare *and* K-12) under one national brand is rare** — only **Amergis, Cross Country, Supplemental Health Care, and Sunbelt** share Soliant's shape.

**Soliant strengths**
- Allied health + school therapy/special-ed placement breadth (long-standing top-3 allied; deep SLP/OT/PT/school-psych/BCBA bench).
- All-50-state reach with teletherapy layered on.
- Joint Commission certified since 2011; mature ~1,200-recruiter org.

**Where competitors lead**
- **Travel-nurse scale:** Aya, AMN, Medical Solutions, CHG dwarf Soliant in nurse revenue.
- **Locum tenens:** CHG (~31% share) + Jackson own physicians/locums.
- **Teletherapy *software*:** Presence, eLuma are platform-native; Soliant offers teletherapy as a *service*, not a software product.
- **Substitutes at scale:** ESS, Kelly — a segment Soliant doesn't play in.
- **Therapy roll-up scale:** TSSG is the larger school-therapy consolidator.
- **Gig/per-diem:** ShiftKey/CareRev's marketplace model is orthogonal to the agency model.
- **Public-market capital:** AMN, Cross Country, Kelly have public currency; Soliant is PE-held (Vistria).

## 4. Ownership map (who's public, who's PE)

Public: **AMN** (NYSE), **Cross Country** (Nasdaq CCRN), **Kelly** (KELYA). PE/private: **Soliant** (Vistria), **TSSG** (Five Arrows), **CHG** (Leonard Green/Ares), **Medical Solutions** (Centerbridge/CDPQ), **Amergis** (Maxim family). Founder-led: **Aya**, **Jackson**. VC: **Swing, ShiftKey, CareRev, Trusted**.
