# Document medical necessity every visit

**Status:** Absolute rule
**Domain:** Documentation / compliance
**Applies to:** `physical-therapy-rehab-clinic`

> Advisory only — not legal or clinical advice. Specific signature/content rules are `[verify-at-use]`.

---

## Why this exists

Medical necessity is established **each visit**, not once at the evaluation. A payor or auditor reads the daily note to decide whether skilled therapy was warranted *that day*. A note that lists modalities but never states the clinical reasoning is the most common medical-necessity denial.

## How to apply

- Open each note with **why skilled therapy was required today** — the clinical reasoning, not the exercise list.
- Tie the visit to a **plan-of-care goal** and record measurable, functional change.
- Avoid boilerplate; "patient tolerated treatment well" establishes nothing.

## Edge cases / when the rule does NOT apply

Never — but the *form* of the necessity statement varies by setting and payor; `[verify-at-use]` the applicable documentation standard.
