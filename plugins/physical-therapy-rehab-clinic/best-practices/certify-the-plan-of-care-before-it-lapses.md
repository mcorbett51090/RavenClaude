# Certify the plan of care before it lapses

**Status:** Absolute rule
**Domain:** Plan of care / compliance
**Applies to:** `physical-therapy-rehab-clinic`

> Advisory only. Certification windows and recert timing are `[verify-at-use]` (payor / CMS, change annually).

---

## Why this exists

The plan of care is a signed instrument with a certification clock. A lapsed certification can invalidate the visits delivered under it — the work was done and documented, but the claim is at risk because the plan was not current.

## How to apply

- Obtain **initial certification** within the required window.
- Track the **recertification clock**; recertify **before** the period lapses or the plan changes materially.
- **Compliance owns the deadline; operations owns the re-book** — hand off early so the recert visit is scheduled in time.

## Edge cases / when the rule does NOT apply

The window length and signature requirements vary by payor/setting; `[verify-at-use]` the current rule.
